Which famous character in Mahabharata was actually Eklavya's incarnation or rebirth?

📅 Sep 08, 2021 09:28 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

SHORT ANSWER: NO ONE

This is a popular modern narrative that Eklavya was reborn as Dhristdyumna the brother of Draupadi to exact avenge from Drona. However, this is an absurd narrative without any foundation.

Both Eklavya and Dhristduymna were contemporaries are were present in the Rajsuy Yagna of Yudhishthira. Eklavya died sometimes between this period and before the start of the Kurukshetra war. Killed by Krishna.

This narrative is nothing but a figment of imagination by someone who never read the epic.

Why is Karna called Mrityunjaya?

Short answer —

  • Authentic epic doesn’t mention Karna as Mrityunjaya.
  • He doesn’t deserve to be called Mrityunjaya
  • Only Shiva deserved to be called Mrityunjaya. No one else.

Why were only Arjuna and Sanjay chosen to hear Gita Upadhesh where as the rest were frozen in time and space?

📅 Mar 28, 2020 01:40 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for A2A

First of all this is an important question, at least the first part (till where).

Why Arjuna?

There are two different answer to this question and both are from two different but relevant aspect.

  1. Gita is not a sermon delivered by Krishna and heard by Arjuna. It is a conversation between Krishna and Arjuna. Arjuna asks and Krishna answers. For such a brilliant answers you need a brilliant question. Without a proper desciple no guru can’t impart there knowledge. So Arjuna receives the answers because he asked, and because he asked the world got blessed with the messages of Gita.
  2. Only Arjuna had the conscience to question the relevance of war. Only he considered that war is an evil. Only he was ready to give up his rights, oaths and even honour. Only he was willing to be a Ranchod like Krishna. In his questions Krishna discovered a true warrior and a worthy candidate for the ancient knowledge which was getting lost!

Why Sanjay?

Because Sanjay was one of the rare person, who considered ethics/dharma over his duty for his King. He questioned him, and chided him for his mistakes and his worthiness was endorsed by Ved Vyas when he granted him the devine power, on Dhritarashtra request. He was another person with morality.

Ved Vyas?

This question is not asked but I would still consider it to be part of the question. Ved Vyas was one of those enlightened people who probably already part of that knowledge which was technically passed by tradition as Krishna mentions in first two verse of Gita. Moreover as he was entrusted to the author the great epic, naturally he must be privy to this conversation.

Why rest were frozen in time

I would not focus too much on the word frozen as it may even be a metaphor rather than actual frozen, although with Lord Krishna around it is not too much of a stretch.

To me frozen implies (both realistically and metaphorically) that others didn’t really benefit from Gita as they were not ready with the right questions and were not interested in those answers. So what if they heard something? Well if they found it irrelevant, they continue to do what they were doing in that span of time — that is they remained frozen in their own time.

Let us understand, while we discuss Mahabharat and Gita here people around are also discussing some beautiful things related to Greek Maythology, Java, Python, Politics. But do we get the benefit from them at this moment? No we are frozen in our own space while others in their own space.

Very often we ourselves chose to be worthy for some great cause and destiny simply endorses our discision. Arjuna chose to be the worthy candidate for Gita.

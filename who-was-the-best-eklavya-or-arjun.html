Who was the best, Eklavya or Arjun?

📅 Apr 28, 2022 11:49 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

Krishna called one of them as his inseparable existence, He killed the other on his way to establish Dharma, guess who was better?

Shiva called one of them as the greatest warrior of the three worlds, other was defeated by Nakul during Rajsuy yagana. Guess who was better?

People often lament Eklavaya might have been a great warrior had his thumbs were not chopped off. While pushing forth this argument they forget —

  • There is a difference between an archer and a warrior. Even with two thumbs Eklavaya may have been a great archer but not a great warrior. He would still have been evil. He would still have been killed. Remember other so called great warriors who turned evil and paid the price.
  • Only one thumb was taken from Eklavaya. Arjuna was Savyasachi (ambidextrous). Was Eklavya too a Savyasachi? If yes, then he wasn’t certainly great with his defeats. If he wasn’t a savyasachi, then you have the answer who was better among the two.

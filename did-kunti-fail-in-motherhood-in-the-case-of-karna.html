Did Kunti fail in motherhood in the case of Karna?

📅 Feb 07, 2021 09:35 AM PST  |  🌐 English

This is an interesting question. Kunti certainly had to let her son go. But unfortunately human psychology and real life challenges aren't simple objective question with a true / false response.

Kunti was helpless when she bore Karna against her own judgement. She bowed down to Suryadev as he threatened to punish Kunti's entire clan, unless she complies. Surya knew Kunti has to abandon him, yet he persisted. I wonder why no one is asking if Suryadev failed Karna as father? Or who was guiltier of the two? Or why he who could have guided Karna without social stigma chose to remain indifferent?

Well let us return to Kunti. She had to give birth to Karna as Surya was adment and threatening. What option did she have?

1.Keep Karna with her.

    • Announce to the world, she is an unwed mother.
    • Bring disgrace to the family.
    • Would any noble king had married her? Do we have any known precedence?
    • How would that have helped Karna?

2. Abandon Karna

  • That was certainly not a favorable option.
  • Karna was Doomed by birth, not by her mother, but by her father.
  • But he still had a chance to a dignified life, much better than the first option.
  • Kunti bargained hard to acquire the most unique gift and ultimate protection for his son — the Kavach and kundal.
  • The basket was designed for safety and comfort.

WHY DIDN'T KUNTI APPROACH KARNA LATER TO DISCLOSE THE SECRET?

Well, Karna was well settled, well educated, and became a king. He was more comfortable than his other sons. There was no reason to disrupt his life and put her to hardship. She must have seen him conspiring against her and her sons but guilt ridden Kunti still couldn't say a word.

Once again that make me wonder, why didn't Surya divulged Karna’s birth facts to him? Why didn’t he explained that it wasn’t Kunti’s fault and that he shouldn’t act in evil manner against his own brothers?

WHY DID KUNTI APPROACH TO KARNA BEFORE THE WAR?

Isn’t it obvious? The tables were about to turn. The happy days of Karna were about to be over, something even Karna was perfectly aware of. She thought her identity and plea would penetrate the stony heart of Karna and he would be compassionate towards his brothers. In turn, that would save the Karna from the sure fate of fighting against Dharma.

But then, Kunti had failed to be mother for Karna. Her inaction had pushed Karna in the camps of evil Duryodhana and he had transformed into the branch of that evil tree. All her plea were futile.

DID KUNTI EVER REGRET?

Kunti regretted. Every moment from the time she invited Surya. She Regretted inviting him. She regretted putting her family in the harms way. She regretted that she had to abandon her infant son. She regretted she wouldn’t be able to love her son. She regretted, she won’t see her son growning.

She must have regretted seeing her sons evil acts. She must have anguished when Karna sent her and her sons to the hous of lac. She must have regretted when Karna abused her daughter in law in open court. She must have regretted everyday that she failed his son. But must have regretted that she had no choice.

But the question is did Surya ever regret? Did Karna ever regret, he failed his mother? Did he ever regret sending her to the house of lac? Guess no one is interested in asking these questions — much easy to blame a hapless woman than the mighty Karna and mighty Surya.

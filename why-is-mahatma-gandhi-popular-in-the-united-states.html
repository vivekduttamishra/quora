Why is Mahatma Gandhi popular in the United States?

📅 Aug 09, 2022 10:31 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

Mahatma Gandhi isn’t popular just in the United States but in every part of the world and in every heart that believe in the goodness of humanity. His name is used as an adjective for peace, truth and non-violence. People can see through what he achieved and were mesmerized to see an alternative way wars could be won — not by fear and destruction, but by vanquishing the hatred in the heart. Any one or nation that values human values have no option but to follow Gandhiji

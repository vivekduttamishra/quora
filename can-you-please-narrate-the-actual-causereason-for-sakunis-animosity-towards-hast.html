Can you please narrate the actual cause/reason for Sakuni's animosity towards Hastinapur and its kingdom? Is it so severe as to destroy the entire Kuru race?

📅 Jul 16, 2020 11:44 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

The actual answer may shock you — Shakuni had no animosity with Hastinapur. He and his families earnestly supported Dhritarashtra and his sons and with their best interest in mind. He was a minor minion in Duryodhan conspiracies most of the time.

His only significant role was in the dice game, where, too, he coined this idea to avoid a war.

He sided with Vudura and others in persuading Duryodhana to accept Krishna's peace offer and avoid the war.

During the war, he and his family fought along side Hastinapur and perished with them. The Kuru clan didn't perish.

Rest is fake.

My answer is based on the story of Mahabharat an Indian epic and refers to authentic versions including —

  • English translation by Kisari Mohan Ganguli (KMG),
  • Bibek Debroy translation of Bori critical edition and
  • Neelkanth edition Sanskrit Hindi version of Gita Press.
  • Mahabharat by Rajgopalachari based on Dakshinayat

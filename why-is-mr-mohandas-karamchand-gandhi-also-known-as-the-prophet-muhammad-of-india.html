Why is Mr. Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi also known as the prophet Muhammad of India?

📅 Apr 04, 2021 07:35 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

EKAM SAT VIPRA BAHUDA VADANTI

The ultimate truth, that is existence (Parmatma) is one people call it by different names.

Those who have any knowledge of Upnisada areaware of this ultimate philosophy of sanatan dharma.

Now consider Mohan Das Karamchand Gandhi and how he has been perceived differently by great men of the world —

  • Lord Mount Batten proclaimed Mahatma Gandhi would go down in history at part with Gautam Buddha and Jeasus Christ.
  • Dr Rajendra Prasad, the first president of India, in his book compared him to the personification of Bhagirathi Ganga.
  • Netaji Shubhash Chandra Bose called him the father of the nation.
  • Their countrymen fondly named called him Mahatma and Bapu.
  • International media called him the man of the century.
  • PM Modi, and Barack Obama, the former president of the USA, calls him his greatest inspiration.
  • Steve Jobs considered his the mascot of his think different campaign.
  • International media uses his name as an adjective for everything good, for peace and for non-violence.
  • Jinnah called him staunch Hindu.
  • Mohan Bhagwat, the head of RSS, too called him a true and staunch Hindu.

Although I have never heard before today, but I won’t really be surprised if someone prefers to call him fondly as the Prophet Muhhamad of India. Gandhiji worked for every Indian. Although his every movement started with a Hindu prayer, the good that he intended wasn’t confined just to Hindus. It was for everyone’s welfare.

May I request the questioner — exactly who called Mahatma Gandhi as the Prophet Muhhamad of India. I would add it to my database and quote in my next talks and articles. I would greatly appreciate that, because honestly I never heard anyone calling Gandhiji with this name.

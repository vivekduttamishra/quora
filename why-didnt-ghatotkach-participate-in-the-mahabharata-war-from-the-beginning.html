Why didn't Ghatotkach participate in the Mahabharata war from the beginning?

📅 May 08, 2020 08:52 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Ghatotkatch did participate in the war from almost the beginning. His participation is mentioned clearly as early as day 4.

Its the modern retelling that introduces them on the day 14. To be fair, Mahabharat war had so many characters involved that it is almost impossible to depict them everywhere.

My answer is based on the story of Mahabharat an Indian epic and refers to authentic versions including —

  • English translation by Kisari Mohan Ganguli (KMG),
  • Bibek Debroy translation of Bori critical edition and
  • Neelkanth edition Sanskrit Hindi version of Gita Press.
  • Mahabharat by Rajgopalachari based on Dakshinayat

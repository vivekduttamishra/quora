Can the Brahmadand neutralize weapons like Pashupatastra, Vaishnavastra and infallible weapons like Vasavi Shakti?

📅 Jul 25, 2020 01:04 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

Pashupatastra is the highest weapon mentioned in the epic. It is that weapon that Shiva uses to annihilate the entire creation at the end of the kalpa, so there is no parallel.

That said these weapons when collided had potential to destroy all life on earth. Who wins the round would be inconsequential as no one would live to tell the tale.

That's the reason why —

  • Krishna didn't ask Pandava to fight Narayanastra.
  • Krishna himself defused Vishnavastra and Asgwattgama's Brahmasta rather than letting them collide.
  • Arjuna never used Pashupatastra.

My answer is based on the story of Mahabharat an Indian epic and refers to authentic versions including —

  • English translation by Kisari Mohan Ganguli (KMG),
  • Bibek Debroy translation of Bori critical edition and
  • Neelkanth edition Sanskrit Hindi version of Gita Press.
  • Mahabharat by Rajgopalachari based on Dakshinayat

What is fake secularism?

📅 Feb 23, 2020 04:36 AM PST  |  🌐 English

It is a global opinion — Secularism in Indian is flawed.

The concept itself is flawed and implemented in the wrong way. The core idea of secularism in political context is separation of state and relegion. That means two things —

  1. state or governance will not interfere in relegious matter.
  2. citizens of India shall be equal before law irrespective of relegion. State will be governed by the law.

But it didn’t happen in India. Government Introduced —

  1. Hindu code bill 1953 with the goal of introducing reforms in Hindu personal law.
  2. There exist a separate Muslim Personal law

Now where is separation of state and subject? Government always felt shy of suggesting Uniform civil code.

To deviate from the matter, the 42 amendment introduced the word secular in the constitution’s preamble that was un-needed.

  • What was our fabric before 42nd amendment?
  • Why should SC participate in Sabrimala debate which is not a state subject?

With constantly changing definition, secularism has become a political weapon of appeasement. If you have nothing to say and confuse the masses, you can use it both as shield and weapon.

Who will win between Joker (DC Comics character) and Shakuni (Mahabharat character) in a dice game?

📅 May 03, 2021 08:24 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

I wouldn’t answer who would win. But I can surely tell you, who would lose.

We would lose. The moment we start to compare and degrade our great epic culture to the level of a comic book, we would lose.

Indian epics are not some comic but a cultural document dating over five thousand years old. And this question open a Pandora box. First Joker and Shakuni? Then what would come next? Thor vs _______?

And all it would take is a writer with skewed sense of creativity and publisher with bigger pocket and our epic would be a joke.

Please stop! Don’t degrade your great ancient civilization that predates all those comic book, their creator, their imaginations, their value system and their civilization by more than five thousand years. In an era, when Shakuni existed, even those nations didn’t exist where the concept of Joker was created.

Please stop! making a mock of yourself!

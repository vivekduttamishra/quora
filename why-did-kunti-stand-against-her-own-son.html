Why did Kunti stand against her own son?

📅 Mar 17, 2022 11:41 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

WRONG QUESTION.

Kunti didn’t stand against any of her son. She always stood for them. She was with them when Bhimasena was poisoned. She was with five of them when the sixth son tried to burn them down.

But I believe the question targets his first son Karna. Kunti was with them even in deserting him. She knew she couldn’t keep Karna with her without jeopardizing even his future and commissioning him to a life of infamy. But then she ensured him a great life by demanding divine protection from Surya and ensuring he is taken well care by entrusting him with wealth.

She didn’t interfere in his life as long as he was happy although Karna did everything to injure his other sons, but she rushed to save him when his destruction appeared imminent.

From where I see, there are fewer woman who could balance the life as daughter, mother and wife.

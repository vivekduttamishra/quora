How many days would it take Barbareeka to end the Mahabharata battle?

📅 Mar 14, 2020 10:26 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Barbareeka, in my understanding, is an overrated character and just like this question is more of a projection than backed by a proven track record.

He is also a wonderful paradox — I will take side with the weaker one! So what if Barbareeka sides with Pandavas first and fights and if he was as great as he said he would probably destroy 70% of Kauravas army and then realize that they are now the weaker side and guess what — he would switch side and start destroying the Pandavas — guess where would that end?

Have you read the story of two cats and a monkey? Well Barbareeka is that monkey! And guess what was the greatest flaw in Barbareeka — there was no sense of justice or dharma here.

It doesn’t matter how much time he would need to finish the war — One shouldn’t be allowed to participate in a war, if he doesn’t have a sense of justice or cause for which he is fighting — He won’t be fighting a war but slaughtering like an animal.

And Krishna did what was right — Remove him from the Dharmayudh!

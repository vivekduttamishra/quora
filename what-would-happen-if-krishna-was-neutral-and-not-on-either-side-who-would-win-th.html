What would happen if Krishna was neutral and not on either side? Who would win the Mahabharata then?

📅 May 12, 2020 04:02 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

In a war to establish Dharma, an incarnation like Krishna doesn't have a choice to be neutral — it defeats the very purpose and spirit of incarnation. Not siding with Dharma would be Adharma. And if Krishna sides with Adharma, guess who would win?

The only situation in which Krishna would have been neutral is if he wasn't an avatar. And if so it won't have mattered even if he has sided with Kauravas. Dharma would have found a channel to win.

Remember, Lord Shiva himself blessed Arjuna. Hanuman graced Pandavas. With divine blessing on the side of Dharma, who would win?

The most potent of all weapon was in the hands of Arjuna. Bheem alone killed all Kaurava's. Arjuna alone vanquished entire Kaurava's army in Virat. Why do you think think, it would be any different.

But that's all hypothetical. Krishna was an incarnation with pronounced responsibility to destroy evil, and establish Dharma. So, as I said there is no room for speculation.

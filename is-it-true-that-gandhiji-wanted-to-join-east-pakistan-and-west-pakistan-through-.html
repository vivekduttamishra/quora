Is it true that Gandhiji wanted to join East Pakistan and West Pakistan through a highway parting India? Why would some people put such accusations on him?

📅 Oct 04, 2021 09:49 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Short Answer: Absurdly NO.

Gandhiji never ever considered such a proposition. Gandhiji who was averse to the idea of India’s partition in the first place was still not just a practical man but a great visionary. He knew the significance of nations security and as such never ever discussed even the possibility of it.

So now coming to the second part of the question —

WHY DO SOME PEOPLE ACCUSE GANDHIJI?

This is not the only accusation that has been levelled against Gandhiji, but one in a series of absurd accusations. And a closer examination of most of them renders them fake, out of context, incomplete or simply ill represented. There are many such stories.

But just because many people know nothing and accuse someone, doesn’t make their point true.

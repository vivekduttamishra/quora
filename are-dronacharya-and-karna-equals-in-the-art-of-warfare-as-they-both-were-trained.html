Are Dronacharya and Karna equals in the art of warfare as they both were trained by the same teacher Parashuram?

📅 Mar 26, 2020 10:08 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

First the foundation of question is illogical.

It has never happened that students of same Guru become equal. A guru is not a factory or McDonals to produce an identical product.

The students should have their own quality? His focus, his practices and his own goal in life. And the first quality a student should have trust and devotion in his Guru. Only then you can learn something from your guru.

Karna was a student of both Drona and Parshurma that should make him better than Drona. Alas!

  • Drona became a teacher and produced a lot of worthy students.
  • Karna’s goal was self glory and accumulation of power. He cheated his one guru insulted his other.

With different goal and conduct two students can’t be equal!

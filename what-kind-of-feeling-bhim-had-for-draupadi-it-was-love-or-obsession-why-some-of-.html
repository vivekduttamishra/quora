What kind of feeling bhim had for Draupadi it was love or obsession? Why some of the Arjun fans are saying bhim helped Draupadi only to show his physical strength?

📅 Feb 04, 2021 11:45 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Over five thousand years ago, Duryodhana thought of hatching a similar conspiracy. He thought, he could create rift among Pandavas using Draupadi or some other woman. The brothers would fight and destroy themselves. He failed. Miserably.

Why did Duryodhana failed? Because they thought they could divide Pandavas. He underestimated their mutual love and respect. Draupadi too played an important role in their unity. Neither the love of Draupadi nor the deceit of Duryodhana could divide them.

Five thousand years later someone is trying to take a page from Duryodhana's diary and again trying to create a rift between Bhima and Arjuna. Little did they realize, the result won't be much different today.

I have not come across any Arjuna fan to have malice against Bhima. Bhima was one of the strong support of Pandavas and always worked for the family. They all worked for common goal complementing each other.

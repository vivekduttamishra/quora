Not withstanding their birth rights, if you peruse the qualities and characters of both Yudhistara and Duryodhana who was the better candidate for taking the crown of Hastinapura? How do you justify your choice?

📅 Mar 20, 2021 05:44 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Good question.

What are the qualities of the character that would make one superior over the other for Kingship?

Who would you like to be your king?

  • One who eliminates dissidents using conspiracy and treacherous ways, or the one who strives for co-existence despite multiple conspiracies against them?
  • One who is so liked by the subjects that they may remove the King and install him as the king, or one who would like to burn down such a favorite choice of the people to install himself as the king?
  • One who earns powerful allies out of his character’s goodness so much, that they respect him even when they are in exile, or one who offers fat bribes and portfolio to earn a powerful ally?
  • One who can transform a barren land into a prosperous kingdom, or one who throws his kingdom into a war that turns it into ruins?
  • A war-monger, who thinks the kingdom is his property or a compassionate person who is ready to forgo his personal right and vengeance to ensure peace?
  • One who runs protecting the dignity and life of another women in distress (Gandharva, Hidimba) or one who tries to rob them out of it?
  • One who protects even his enemies or one who backstabs even his own relatives?
  • One whose brothers are like his strengths, complement him and even fight him if he is wrong, or one who brothers are just like stooge to him?
  • One who would fight Dharma to get his brothers back or one who would send all his brothers to death for his ambition.
  • One who is supported by Krishna, or the other supported by evil conspirators in his venture?

Choice is more than clear. You just need to ask the right question. Now your turn — Not withstanding their birth rights, if you peruse the qualities and characters of both Yudhistara and Duryodhana who was the better candidate for taking the crown of Hastinapura?

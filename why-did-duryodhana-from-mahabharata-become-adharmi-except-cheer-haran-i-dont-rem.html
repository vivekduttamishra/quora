Don’t worry if you don’t remember any of this. I am here to help you —

  1. While still an adolescent, he poisoned Bhima. Tried to kill a someone. Do you think it wasn’t a sin?
  2. He tries to burn his cousins and aunt alive in a lakshagrih, just to be the king. Was that innocence?
  3. In Draupadi Swyamber, having failed to win he tries to burn the princess alive for chosing a Brahmin over a kshatriya. Can you compare this incident to an acid attack or worst?
  4. He manipulates his father in giving Khandavprast a poor bargain. Is dishonesty considered a sin?
  5. He tries to rob Pandavas of their kingdom by deceitful game. Pandavas were fool accepted. But here we are talking about Duryodhana. Right?
  6. I will not touch upon chir-haran, as question already acknowledges that.
  7. He again manipulates his father, to force Pandava for another game of dice and again by deciet sends them to exile or 13 years.
  8. He refuses any peacetalk even one unreasonably skewed in his favour and forced a war that could have been avoided.

How many more would you like to count.

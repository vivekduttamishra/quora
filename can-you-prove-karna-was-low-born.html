Can You prove Karna was Low Born?

📅 Dec 02, 2021 10:08 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Short answer: NO

Here we are attempting to prove what doesn’t really exist.

Karna was born to Kunti and Surya. Niether were by wildest imagination “low” on anything.

But perhaps, the question seeks the credential of his foster parents. They were Suta. And sutas weren’t ‘low’ by any margin. There were people born of Kshatriya and Brahman reunion, which can’t be considered ‘low’.

Sutas were known to have occupied some prominent posistion in the society including being ministers, rishis and generals. Sanjay, a suta, on ocassion acted as ambassador of Hastinapur. Keechak, another suta, was the army general of Matasya and the famous suta Karna was the KIng of Anga.

You may not have missed the absence of caste in my answer. The reason being the word is also absent in the epic. Vidura the son of a maid of Ambika was the prime minister of Hastinapur. While the son of a fisher woman was the most renowned rishi of the time. A closer look will reveal there was no caste based exploitation in the epic era.

Who was born to win? Was it Arjuna, Karna, Duryodhana or Yudhishtira?

📅 Feb 26, 2021 06:34 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

God created us all with infinite opportunity — opportunity to succeed, opportunity to show compassion, opportunity to wreak havoc, opportunity to do good, opportunity to fall prey to evil desires.

What God doesn’t do is control our action. They are controlled by our gunas — sato, Rajo and Tamas. We are the sum of our action — victory or defeat is the consequence.

Those who control their gunas are victorious and sucessful. Those who are controlled by their gunas are failures.

And if failures are powerful, they are diestrous — Ravana, Hitler. But they don’t win. Neither battles nor hearts.

So the right question is not who was born to win rather it should be —

WHO WAS WORTHY TO BE VICTORIOUS?

And this question is easy to answer. Ask anyone from the era — Parshurama, Bhishma, Drona, Karna, Ashwatthama, Kripacharya, Vidura, Krishna and even Mahadeva. They all have answered this question at some point or other. And they have answered this question with conviction and without an iota of suspicion.

Finally, I believe you know the answer too. Let me give you a hint.

Destiny and divinity favoured his victory

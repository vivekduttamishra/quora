Did Draupadi regret not accepting Karna?

📅 Oct 20, 2021 11:50 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

Yes she did regret for not accepting Karna several times —

  • She regretted in several TV Shows.
  • She regretted in several novels.
  • She still regrets everyday in the hearts of thousands of Karna fans.

But that are the recent regrets. The real Draupadi from over five thousand years ago, had no reason to regret. All the evidences clearly point she never rejected Karna who simply failed to fulfil the terms of swyamver. But let us set this story aside for a while, and just for once to please those fans, assume Draupadi did reject Karna. After all in retrospection, Draupadi may have had relished the idea of rejecting that wicked man.

Don’t you think, she would have been proud of her decision for the rest of her life? Can you think of one reason she should have regretted?

  • Who would regret not choosing a person as a husband who would try to burn her alive for having failed to secure her matrimony? She may have regretted had Karna taken a stand to protect her and her family from attackers who were trying to hurt them. She may even have regretted had Karna succeeded in actually hurting her. But who would regret not marrying a loser who fled scared?
  • Who would regret not marrying a molester who tries to disrobe a woman in public and abuses her with choicest of word that no civic person dares to speak? I am sure Karna’s real wife must have regretted hearing his deeds. Krishna certainly proclaimed Karna’s wives would weep at their fate for his evil actions. And they did weep.
  • Who would regret not marrying a man, who would fled enemies living women of the family behind? Don’t you think women who were captured by Gandharvas must have regretted marrying cowards who fled leaving them behind?

So No, beyond the wild imaginations of few fans, Draupadi never regretted not marrying Karna and unfortunately never rejected him. But I think, she had several reasons to imagine if she had a chance and she could have rejected him, over and over again!

Is Arjuna really a coward because he begged lord Shiva for Pashupatastra to match Karna in the Mahabharata?

📅 Feb 22, 2020 01:10 PM PST  |  🌐 English

Interesting question —

Arjun didn't beg of Pashupatastra nor Shiva granted alms to a beggar. He earned it rightfully by worshipping Shiva and proving his worthiness. So Arjun earned it is the right word.

Karna didn't earned his weapon — he stole it by lying to his guru.

Earn (or as question says beg) vs steal — what you think is better?

Was Arjun afraid? May be. Won't it be dumb to go to a battle unprepared?

It is like when India buys a new weapon system from say USA you say — India cowardly begged (not bought) USA for weapons against Pakistan.

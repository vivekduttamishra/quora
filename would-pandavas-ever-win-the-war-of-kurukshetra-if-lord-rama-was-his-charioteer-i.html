Would Pandavas ever win the war of Kurukshetra if Lord Rama was his charioteer instead of Lord Krishna?

📅 Nov 14, 2020 09:19 PM PST  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

The answer to the question is an undoubted YES.

People asking this question (and this question has been asked again and again) forget Rama and Krishna are quintessentially same. Their actions may appear different, but thats because of the era they belonged.

Let us try to answer this question first by seeing how Rama and Krishna are exactly the same —

  • Both Rama and Krishna tried their best to avoid the war in every possible way they could. While Rama sent Angad as an emmissary, Krishna himself was the emmissary.
  • They both knew virtue or restraint. Rama could have dried up the ocean by invoking a celestial weapon. He didn’t. Krishna idolized Rama when he says — among warriors, I am Rama. Krishna could have fought the battle, instead he chose to become a guide.
  • They both also knew the importance of demonstrating power under the restraint — Krishna showed his virat swaroop, and ocean got scared of a furious Rama.
  • For those who understand the essence of Dharma, they don’t get into un-necessary details. So those who think Krishna was cunning and Rama couldn’t have done what Krishna did should remember Vali Vadh. Does that make Rama, cunning at par with Krishna? No that is a reminder that neither were cunning — just the most brilliant strategist who were not fooled by the dialogs of Shakuni of Start Plus — Jo dharm ke sath hoten hai unke sath bahut adharm kia ja sakta hai — No it is a stupid dialog and doesn’t define Rama or Krishna.

Now let us understand why people doubt Rama’s effectiveness at Kurukshetra — Rama was not cunning as Krishna — As I said, neither Rama, nor Krishna were cunning. They were streategist and there is a huge difference between the two terms — one sides with dharma, while other with Adharma. When time comes Krishna.

But imagine this! Rama formed a victorious army out of vanars. What he couldn’t have achieved with an army lashed with all the celestial weapons?

First let me try answering this question, no matter, how stupid the question may be.

Arjuna never defeated Hanuman. Arjuna never dreamt of Defeating Hanuman. There is not a single reference exists anywhere in authentic to this effect, as suggested by the question.

Bajrangbali Hanuman is not just bali (powerful) is baldhaam (powerhouse) and it considered as an incarnation of Shiva. He can’t be defeated. But more important Arjuna was not insolent enough to do so. Arjuna’s relation to Krishna was exactly similar to Hanuman’s relation to Rama. Like Hanuman, Arjuna was the greatest devotee, and just as Hanuman was most favorite of Rama, Arjuna was the most favourite of Krishna. In fact both Rama and Krishna said so for thier devotees. Rama said if someone worships Hanuman, he worships me. No wonder we have tradition of flag worship at Hanuman temple at Ramnavmi. Similarly Krishna on multiple occassion suggested — He who hates Arjuna, hates me. He who loves Arjuna loves me.

Second, both Hanuman and Bhima are Pavan Putra. By that relationship, Hanuman is elder brother to Arjuna. Bhima requested Hanuman to bless Arjuna. There was no conflict.

No. You are not right.

BORI is not considered fake, rather critical. It as compared over a thousand manuscripts and removed interpolations. That also mean that BORI is not the only one to say this. In fact there are just 4 manuscript out of 800+ which made the claim that Draupadi forbid Karna from participating.

But yes there are over a 1000 answers on quora that try hard to support these theories (few answers exists for this question as well)

The new arguments for which even BORI doesn’t have answer includes

  1. Karna mentioned by BORI is not the same Karna
  2. Karna was denied an entry to Swaymber by Drupad ← Not Draupadi
  3. Karna never participated.
  4. Failure of Karna mentioned related to another Karna possibly one of the Kaurava brother with same name
    1. Truth is no Kaurava brother named Karna was so popular as to be named explicity
  5. But somehow Draupadi still stopped him ← Maybe he was not participating and Draupadi said — “hey you, I know you are not allowed, but if you try, I will not marry you”.

So what is the truth

  • There are over 800 manuscript that BORI consulted. And it
    • rejects Draupadi forbidding Karna theory.
      • It was present in 4 out of 800+ manuscripts

      • There are contradictions to this story (next point)

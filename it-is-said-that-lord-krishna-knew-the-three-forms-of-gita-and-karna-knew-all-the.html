It is said that Lord Krishna knew the three forms of Gita and Karna knew all the forms of Gita. Was Karna more knowledgeable than Lord Krishna?

📅 Aug 13, 2020 04:34 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

But you really don't expect to answer this question. Do you?

How can I even know the answer? I know less Gita than Krishna, am less knowledgeable than him. How would I judge someone more knowledgeable than him?

So let's ask one who is considered most knowledgeable — Karna.

What does he say?

No. He doesn't say anything —

  • He never boasts of anything more than his archary skills.
  • He doesn't talk about Karma yoga or Bhakti yoga.
  • He never shows a Virata Swaroop.
  • He had no understanding of niskama Karma.
  • He had no devotion to anyone including his guru.

He never showed by word or action that he had any knowledge of Gita.

I don't know why people want to make fun of Karna by asking such question ridiculing him. They can't be his wellwishers.

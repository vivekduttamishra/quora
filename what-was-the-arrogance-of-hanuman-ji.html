What was the arrogance of Hanuman Ji?

📅 Apr 20, 2020 11:22 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Hanuman's arrogance, if we can call it, was his unconditional devotion to Lord Ram — that Ram's greatness and power has no limits. But since Rama's greatness knew no bound, it was a fact and can't be considered as arrogance.

Otherwise Hanumanji is the humblest among Gods and warriors.

As a kid, he is said to have misused his untamed powers such as gulping the Surya. One such misadventure earned him a curse blocking his power. But these were a childish act and not arrogance.

My answer is based on the story of Ramayana an Indian epic. I have taken reference from Balmiki Ramayan and Ramcharit Manas.

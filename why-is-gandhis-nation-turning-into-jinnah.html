Why is Gandhi's nation turning into Jinnah?

📅 Mar 11, 2020 08:17 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Sadly Gandhi's nation is turning into Jinnah's because we have started asking these questions

  • How is it Gandhi's nation?
  • When did Gandhi got entire nation registered in his name?
  • Nation is not Gandhi's Daady's property.

Do you see the disease these questions carry in its womb? No its not just a naked undiluted hatred for the man whose contributions surpasses at least everyone present in this forum.

The main disease is they consider the country to be a property — something to be owned, and not something to be dedicated to.

Bhagat Singh kissed the gallows not fighting any property title suite, Chandrashekhar Azad killed himself with his own bullet fighting a war that wasn't about ownership of nation but for the pride of calling India a free nation. Do you know the dying thoughts of Asfaqullah Khan —

जाऊँगा खाली हाथ मगर ये दर्द साथ ही जाएगा,

जाने किस दिन हिंदुस्तान आजाद वतन कहलाएगा?

Pay attention it was not ownership but dedication.

I without having any significant contribution to the nation can proudly proclaim India is my country!

The problem is the thought of this ownership that prompted Jinnah to demand a separate land to rule — divide the nation.

Now we are out with same question of ownership how much portion does Gandhi own of India? And how much Bose and Patel own? Do you realize by the arithmetic of ownership —

gandhi+Bose +Patel +Bhagat Singh + (1000s others) =100%

Now for the sake of debate (and out of hatred) even if we make Gandhi's share 0, what about shares of Bose and Patel? Say 1/million of a %? Negligible? Do you see the disease that reduces national heros and their contributions to nothingness.

Let's rethink we don't own land but the pride of calling India as My India — then I have national pride of 100%, so will have Gandhi and Bose and Azad? Now how much of Indian pride we have accumulated among 125 million living and multitude Indians gone by and yet to come?

Why divide when you can multiply?

The division is Jinnah's legacy and the question — How is it Gandhi's nation? is turning it into Jinnah's nation.

Jinnah is not Gandhi's creation its a side effect of distortions of Gandhi's ideals.

But people opposing CAA is certainly subscribing to Jinnah's ideal in equal proportion to people who are dividing India's legacy by dividing Bose and Gandhi like heros.

And this disease is more deadly than the Corona virus currently trembling the world.

The second part of the question is WRONG! Everyone who knows Krishna and Hanuman helped Arjuna in Gandharva battle of Matasya battle knows nothing of Mahabharata.

But anyhow, I believe the questioner already knows Arjuna’s great feat in both Gandharva battle and Virata battle and want to avoid the topic hoping Arjuna may be left with no great feat. But Arjuna’s greatness isn’t related to these two battles. So let us get started.

1.How Arjuna became Drona’s favorite pupil

Arjuna wasn’t intended to be Drona’s favorite pupil, Ashwatthama was. In fact Drona never wanted Arjuna to learn the art of fighting at night. But with his perseverance and dedication, Arjuna won his gurus heart. Once Drona was taking a bath in river when an alligator attacked him. Students rushed. But all of them were just moot spectator to what would have been a great tragedy. Everyone except Arjuna. He killed the alligator and protected his guru. All while he was just a young boy.

2.Gurudakshina Battle

It was Arjuna who captured Drupada in this great battle that was undertaken at his Guru’s behest. BORI has condensed the entire story two just two paragraph. The first paragraph gives a confusing view that all Kaurava’s and Pandavas won this battle together. Not only this fact is in contradiction to more elaborate discription present in Neelkantha and other ancient scripts, but the second reference to this incident even in BORI credits Partha for this victory.

But what makes this a real great feat wasn’t the victory but the fact that of all pupil of Drona, Arjuna alone had promised to fulfil guru’s desire even before the start of learning, while he was just a young lad and had no idea of what guru would ask. That is called devotion.

3.Swyamvara Battle

Arjuna was victorious in Draupadi’s swyamvara where Kings like Shalya and Karna failed. But the arrogant Kings couldn’t digest their failure and decided to destroy the Panchala royal family by killing Drupada and his sons and throwing Draupadi back into fire. As they proceeded to put an action to their cruel plan, Arjuna intercepted them. He wasn’t carrying any special weapon. Yet he decided to do his duty as a Kshatriya. He faced Karna who fought violently with him desiring to kill the Brahmana, because they thought it is permissible to kill a Brahman eager to fight. The only proplem was they didn’t had what it takes to fight a Brahmana who looked like Indra, Vishnu and Parshurama. It scared Karna who retreated thinking he can’t defeat his opponent. He was right. He never could. Arjuna allowed him to retreat graciously.

4. Nivatakavacha

Arjuna assisted Indra and killed their arch rival Nivatakavacha and millions of demons that were part of the army. No other warrior showed any feat nearly as close. Krishna wasn’t with him in this battle.

5. Gandharva battle

Yes, I know I said, and I won’t speak anything about Arjuna’s feat. Just wanted to remind neither Krishna nor Hanuman was there. Not even a passing by reference. Every other warrior from Bhishma to Drona to Ashwatthama constantly remembered Arjuna’s feat.

6. Matasya Battle

Some people say Arjuan was supported by Hanumana here. WRONG! Arjuna had a chariot with flag bearing the mark of Hanumana. It was the same banner that Karna proclaimed he would cut and throw on ground. Naturally Karna couldn’t have dared to say the same for Hanumana. But… You see, I kept my word of not showing Arjuna’s valor. But I can’t stop Bhishma, Drona, Ashwatthama and Karna from remembering this heroic of Arjuna. Can I?

7. Dwarka Robbers

Krishna had finished his stay on earth. Arjuna’s divine weapons too were gone. What was left was a devastated Dwarka with wailing women, children and old people. Arjuna took them into his custody. A gang of robbers ambushed them. What did Arjuna do? Did he flee scared leaving them behind to save his own life? Some other warriors of the might have done just fled to save their lives. No he continued to fight them using how bow as mere iron rod. Robbers had to retreat. Although they did manage to take many women along with them, Arjuna managed to save most of them including Queens of Krishna. He went on to rehabilitate them across Aryavart.


MOST IMPORTANT FEATS OF ARJUNA

A. Grace of Krishna and Hanuman

The question assumes that having the blessing of Hanuman or Krishna was some kind of taboo that one should be ashamed of. Actually it is other way round. What could be a greater feat that divinity like Krishna and Hanuman would come forward to help you in your endeavor? Can you imagine a great feat one can exhibit? Arjuna won their favor because they saw something in Arjuna that they couldn’t find in anyone else.

Every other warrior cherished divine weapons or mighty army. Some traded their soul to lay their hands on one such divine weapon. But Arjuna won the heart of divinity. And that in my opinion is the greatest feat of all.

B. Arjuna Lost to Lord Shiva

Lord Shiva himself came to test Arjuna’s perseverance in the form of a Kirata. Obviously no one could win against Shiva. Arjuna was no match. He was badly thrashed. But he didn’t retreat. He didn’t flee. He would have died, but continued to face one who has no parallel in the universe. This earned him the grace of Shiva! Shiva gave him Pashupata which was incomprehensible to everyone including Gods. And that is a feat with no parallel comparison.

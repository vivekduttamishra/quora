Who'll win a fight between Vishwamitra and the Pandava army (with Krishna) ?

📅 May 20, 2020 02:43 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Ha, several questions on this genre float on quora — let to unlikely characters from epic fight each other and predict the outcome like it is some wrestling match for fun — and they get to see this fight for free on Quora. But they miss one important point. Epic is not a mindless wrestling sport. So the main question always is why would they fight, because they will not fight without a reason. Let us first introduce the new entrant in this arena —

Vishwamitra

Vishwamitra is an epic in himself, and there are fewer example of more brave person than him. He is Ram’s guru, and he blessed Rama with a majority of Divyastra he possessed. Vishwamitra is the epic tale of King Vishwaratha journey from an arrogant King to that of brahmarishi. With his capability he created a new heaven for Trishanku, bestowed great Lord Ram with celestial weapons. But let us understand —

Vishwamitra was no warrior

  • Vishwamitra is the identity of a Rishi and not a warrior. He had renounced all his wars and that is why he sought out Ram to fight Tadka and Marich.
  • King Vishwaratha was a warrior, but he too was defeated.
  • Vishwamitra victory was over his own ego and a motivation to achieve greatness of character, very few achieved. But he wasn’t a warrior with weapons. He had left that role a lifetime ago.

Why would they fight?

  • Vishwamitra refused to fight even demons like Tadka and Marich. What greater crime would Pandavas (with Krishna) commit to incurr the wrath Vishwamtira that he would giveup his character and resort to war?
  • When Vishwamitra created an alternate heaven for Trishanku, he lost his tapa. So if he does it again…

No two people on side of Dharma wouldn't fight and you will not see this WWE match.

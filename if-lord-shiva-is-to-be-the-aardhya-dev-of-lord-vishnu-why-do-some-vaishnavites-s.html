If Lord Shiva is to be the Aardhya Dev of Lord Vishnu, why do some Vaishnavites still refuse to accept the fact that Lord Visnu is a devotee of Lord Shiva?

📅 Mar 12, 2022 09:35 PM PST  |  🌐 English

I think, it is exactly because of —

  • It is because few people with pity and narrow mindset are trying to use Shiva and Vishnu as their horses in a racecourse.
  • A question like this, which is derogatory for Santana philosophy and insulting to almighty Shiva. Yes, it doesn’t please Shiva when someone disrespects Vishnu. And a proof exists in the story of Kagbhushandi’s story.
  • It is because they don’t realize Brahma Vishnu Sadashiva janata Aviveka.
  • Shiva is the all prevailing, all mighty, the God of Gods. He exists in every single being. Shivoham — I am Shiva. Then he must exists even in Vishnu. How can some one belittle Vishnu without belittling Shiva.
  • Shiva doesn’t seek devotees who are ignorant of Dharma and trying to axe the basic believe of Santana culture where Vishnu occupies a revered position.

There are various sacred texts, some claim Shiva and some claim Vishnu supreme. They refer to the entity Parambrahma. Fools differentiate. But few understand the hidden truth that the un-manifested supreme power can manifest in any form — as Shiva, as Vishnu as Devi. When we can’t differentiate, it is our limitation and not the limitation of Devadhideva Anadi Anatant Shiva, or Adi Narayana.

If you are a gambler, put your bait on horses. Don’t defile Santana culture by such derogatory remarks and narrow mindset. We the devotees of Shiva and the devotees of Vishnu should stand together to protect our Dharma from external invaders and not divide ourselves.

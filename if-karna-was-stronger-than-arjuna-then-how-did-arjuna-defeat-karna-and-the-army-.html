If Karna was stronger than Arjuna then how did Arjuna defeat Karna and the army of Duryodhana during the battle of Matsya prior to the Kurukshetra War?

📅 Feb 25, 2020 12:41 AM PST  |  🌐 English

The answer lies in your question — In fact the very first word — if.

That is a wishful thinking many carry after watching numerous TV shows and books with him as protagonist — where he is shown both a victim and larger than himself — his vices censored, his misfortune glorified. But what does Mahabharat text says —

  1. He fled from Gandharvas attack, leaving Duryodhan behind. Arjun defeated Gandharvas and freed him.
  2. Arjun indeed defeated not once but twice in Virat war as you mentioned.
  3. Karna failed to stop Abhimanyu alone.
  4. Karna failed to stop Arjun from reaching Jaidradh.

There is not a single encounter that goes in favour of if Karna was stronger…

How did Eklavya become a superior archer to Arjuna?

📅 May 15, 2020 11:27 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Did he? When? Which war did he won? What warriors he vanquished?

Honestly, the only claim to fame Eklavya has, is his thumb was chopped off. And people simply concluded that if Drona did it, it must be because he was great. But there isn't really any hard evidence backing his greatness, forget being superior.

One great performance in the initial stage doesn't guarantee life long consistency. Unfortunate as Eklavya was, doesn't make him a great archer.

My answer is based on the story of Mahabharat an Indian epic and refers to authentic versions including —

  • English translation by Kisari Mohan Ganguli (KMG),
  • Bibek Debroy translation of Bori critical edition and
  • Neelkanth edition Sanskrit Hindi version of Gita Press.
  • Mahabharat by Rajgopalachari based on Dakshinayat

📅 Oct 31, 2020 11:53 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

While I disagree with the word — most — I agree with the broader question in general. While it beats me, once again, I can understand it. And there are few reasons for it —

  1. Most important reason. They haven’t read the history. They haven’t followed Gandhiji ideals. They formed their views basing them on social media.
  2. There is a systemetic propaganda at a very high-level to defame Mahatma Gandhi. It is at such a high level that even our Prime Minister Narendra Modi, an adherent follower, is unable to curtail it. Why else such destractors who he himself confessed can’t forgive still sit around him?
  3. This propaganda is trying to defame brand ‘Gandhi’ not realizing —
    1. There is no coorelation between Mahatma and the Gandhi of today. But they think to condemn contemporary Gandhi they need to condemn the Mahatma.
    2. By destroying the Brand Mahatma, that has a global appeal, they are also damaging the Brand India and its credibility.
  4. The propaganda is brain-washing their followers that our history is biased and has overrated Gandhi. And the brain-washed youths don’t realize —
    1. The history of 20th century is an international history, which can’t be controlled by any single entity, not even by a nation. The world knows it, the world has captured it in video camera and news-papers.
    2. People like Albert Einstien, Steve Jobs and Barak Obama haven’t read Indian history to become Gandhi’s fan. And they are Gandhi’s fan. They have no obligation for Indian government or affiliation to any Indian political party to praise Gandhi for personal gain.
    3. People like Dalai Lama were refused support by Indian government against China, yet he is follower of Gandhi. Can’t be politicial. Can it be?
    4. Dr Rajendra Prasad, the first President of India was not a good political term with our current Congress family. But he was a Gandhi’s contemporary and copared Gandhji with Bhagirathi Ganga in his book.
    5. Already mentioned Prime Minister Narendra modi and his reverence for Mahatma Gandhi
  5. Our youths don’t see the whole picture, just few skewed and broken images those propaganda project on the social media.

It is like Kalidasa, trying to cut the branch Indian heritage where you sit and exist.

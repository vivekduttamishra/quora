Will I end up in jail if I refused to call Gandhi as Mahatma or the father of the nation?

📅 Apr 16, 2022 01:43 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

No. Most certainly you won't.

You don't have a Hitler or Putin as your Father of the nation. You have a Mahatma, whom nation fondly called Bapu.

You won't be jailed even if you abuse him, tarnish his character or burn his effigy. You won't be jailed because he went to jail again and again, and yet again to ensure, you don't have to go to jail.

He wasn't the only one who fought for us, went to jail and died for us. But he was one of them that deserves our unconditional reverence. He was one among those who stood in frontline and didn't plead for mercy.

But don't worry. Condemn him if you wish. You won't be jailed. He didn't fight for the country to hear Mahatama or father of the nation from us. He fought and died that we can live in peace and freedom. You are free…

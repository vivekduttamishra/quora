I would agree. Arjuna was nothing without their blessings.

Truth be told, no one is anything or any good without them —

Entire Kuru Army crumbled. Before them mighty Ravan crumbled, Bali destroyed, Hiranyakashyap shredded to pieces, Daksha Prajapati lost his head. And what is the common thread here? Weren't they all might and powerful and supposedly undefeatabke? So what happened? God's abandoned them? No, they abandoned the God.

Ravan failed to see Shiva in Hanuman, Hiranyakashyap didn't realize omnipresent God's protection, Kauravas failed to see Narayana in Krishna, Karna fore shook the stretched hand of Krishna. And rest is, well the epic.

And Arjuna? Well Arjuna surrendered. To surrender to God, you need to surrender to Dharma. He may have lost like other heros of the Kuru side but he won over Shiva and Krishna — with his devotion, the only thing that effects the indomitable Mahadev.

So, yes he may have been nothing like everyone else, but the question is

Why did Shiva and Krishna and everyone else supported Arjuna and Arjuna alone?

  • Shouldn't that be the real question? Shouldn't Arjuna at least get the credit for being their favorite?
  • Arjuna got Pashupatastra from Mahadev, Karna Brahnastra from Parshuram. Do you see the difference in their approach — deceit vs devotion? That was Arjuna's strength?
  • What did Duryodhana desired Krishna or Narayani Sena? Didn't he got what he desired? Didn't he get immense power? That's why it is said Be careful what you wish for. Don't we see Arjuna choose wisely?

The epic has a clear message — weapons don't win wars, neither arrogance nor brute strength. They eventually fade. With Dharma on their Side a kid like Prahlad can defeat Hirankashyap, a weak Sugriv can win against might Bali, waman can dwarf Bali (राजा बली) arrogance.

…And we are talking about mighty Arjuna —

  • He was in paralleled warrior in the testimony of Drona, Bhishma, Bhagwan Parshuram, and above all Mahadev Shiva himself.
  • Who single handedly, defeated entire Kuru Army in Virat.
  • Who defeated Drupad and Gandharvas large army with the help of his brothers alone.

But should you ignore all his great achievements, you can't ignore the context of this question — Mahadev, Krisha, Hanuman and everyone else supported him, and who can be more powerful in the universe?

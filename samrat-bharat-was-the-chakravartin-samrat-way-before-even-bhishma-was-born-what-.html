Just a correction — Yudhishthir didn’t embark for Digvaijaya — he performed Rajsuy yagna. But yes, the intent was same.

Remember, Yudhshithir didn’t rule Hastinapur, but Indraprastha. And Indraprastha was never an empire. After the division of Hastinapur, even Hastinapur was no empire. No King ruled Hastinapur — just a caretaker king. Now generation old emperorship no more applied to either of the two factions. Dhritrashtra not being a true King, couldn’t have performed Rajsuy yagana. On the other hand, Yudhishthir needed to establish himself as a acknowledged ruler of a newly created kingdom.

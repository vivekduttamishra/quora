If Arjun is born now, what would he be doing?

📅 Aug 26, 2021 08:56 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

People always do deeds based on their character traits. If Arjuna would be born today, he would still be doing something for the welfare of humanity. He would still be the brave warrior from the epic who would be invanquishable and incorruptible at the same time — A warrior not inclined to show his powers, a warrior willing to laydown his weapons out of compassion and yet a warrior who could win the battle for righteous reasons.

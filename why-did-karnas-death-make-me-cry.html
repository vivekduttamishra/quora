Why did Karna’s death make me cry?

📅 Nov 22, 2022 08:53 PM PST  |  🌐 English

Let me run some diagnosis on you to determine the reason of such a reaction? Answer these questions in yes or no.

  1. Did you know Karna was taught by Drona?
  2. Did you know Karna was one of the leads of Kings who tried to burn Draupadi?
  3. Did you know Karna was the one who asked Duhsashana to disrobe Drauapdi?
  4. Did you know Karna was the one who suggested Duryodhana to come and mock Pabdavas sufferings in forest?
  5. Did you know Karna fled Gandharvas deserting his friend Duryodhana?
  6. Did you know Karna let his brother get killed at the hand of Arjuna while he filed?
  7. Did you know Karna refused to fight in Kurukshetra till Bhishma is alive because he felt he wouldn't get the credit of his kills?
  8. Did you know Karna fled as Abhimanyu killed his other brother?
  9. Did you know Karna attacked unarmed chariotless Abhimanyu, and Bhima?
  10. Did you know when Karna's was stuck on ground, he wasn't weaponless. He died fighting?

Now count your yes.

  • If you have less than 3 yes, you are misguided by fictions. Reconsider facts. Stop crying for that extremely wicked soul.
  • If you have more than 7 yes, you know you are crying for someone who doesn't deserve your tears. But then, there is no cure.
  • If you feel like contradicting all points, you are blind Karna fan. No cure, again.

Why do Mahabharat TV serials never show about the Gandharva Wars?

📅 May 18, 2020 12:17 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Mahabharat by BR Chopra has the Gandharvas war episode although it was diluted a bit to built a face saving for one of the characters. It is for the very same reason recent shows chose to skip that episode as it upsets their narration and raises uncomfortable questions. That is the same reason Virat war and Gurudakshina war is also reinvented. That is the problem when you lie at one point, you need more and more lies to cover them. This is brilliant case study.

Guess who that character is?

  • He is a student of Drona, whom TV shows chose to hide with fake narrative.
  • He failed at Gurudakshina war, BRChopra even showed it, but modern show again built a narrative to prevent him from going into the war.
  • He naturally failed at Virat war and no narrative can be given, so let us skip the episode.
  • In Virat war was defeated twice, but then another narratives tried to save him again.

Always wonder what's the point in trying so hard to glorify someone that we have to distort the entire epic? What legacy are we passing to our next generation who believe whatever the shows shows them. Is there no responsibility?

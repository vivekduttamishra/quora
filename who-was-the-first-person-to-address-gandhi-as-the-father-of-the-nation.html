Who was the first person to address Gandhi as the father of the nation?

📅 Jun 15, 2020 04:33 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

It is an interesting question. There are a few things which don't have an original date. It was Netaji Shubhash Chandra Bose who first used the word Bapu meaning father for Gandhiji. Over years Indians of that era called him Bapu. By the time India won freedom, he was well known as Bapu and Mahatma. The word eventually got translated as The Father of the Nation, and found its way in the original copy of the constitution through this image.

Notice the text on the left margin.

It is important to note, that it is not a constitutional position, rather a popular salutation that was coined and liked by the masses of the time. And Mahatma remains the father of the nation for all times to come.

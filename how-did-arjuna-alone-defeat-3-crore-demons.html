How did Arjuna alone defeat 3 Crore demons?

📅 Feb 24, 2020 05:35 AM PST  |  🌐 English

This is the kind of question I would rather not answer, despite having reference is some texts as pointed by other participants.

Without aiming to offend any one 3 crores appears to be a exaggerating the definition of exaggeration. There is not likely to be 3cr demons unless we are talking about flies.

Let us understand while Mahabharat happened over 5000 years ago, it's written scripts are not as ancient and for a long time remained verbal based on memory and creativity of story tellers.

That said the divyastra may have achieved such a fit. Yet in my understanding the figures are not important, the idea is.

Such a focus on figures and exaggerated details often make critics redicule the entire Mahabharat as a fable. Let's not give them any fodder.

Oh! I would say it is an old and dated census. Today the population is well over several billion.

You need to understand Sanatan Hindu Phiolosphy — The God is all prevailing, omnipresent. There isn’t anything in the world that is devoid of God, something Hirankashyapa failed to realize at his peril and God manifested from the pillar to put an end to his pitiable existence. We see God in stones and in river, in trees and animals. We have Kuldevta (Family diety), Gram Devta (Village Diety). So with the notion of those personal Gods the population is beyond your imagination. It is a different matter that they all manifest from and emerge back into one supreme Shiva.

But isn’t 33 koti means 33 categories?

What the don’t understand is even 33 would be a large number to redicule someone and feel rediculed if you don’t understand the true sanatana dharma.

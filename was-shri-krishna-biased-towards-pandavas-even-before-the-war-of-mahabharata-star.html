Was Shri Krishna biased towards Pandavas even before the war of Mahabharata started?

📅 Apr 14, 2020 10:11 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for A2A.

Yes, Shri Krishna was biased. He was biased for —

  • Dharma. Anything else and you need to re read Mahbahrat, at least Gita.
  • Arjuna, the only warrior, he knew had the conscience to question the need of war.
  • Making Yudhisthir king, him who sent his brothers the harms way to protect someone who conspired against them the entire life. Remember Gandharva war.
  • Kunti, who was ready to sacrifice his own son to protect their host's family from Bakasur.
  • Draupadi, who freed her husbands from slavery after great gamble, despite the fact that they put her as a bait.
  • Pandavas who despite of getting all celestial powers honored their term of exile. And for peace were ready to forgo their personal vengeance.

Krishna was also biased against —

  • Those who could conspire to burn a entire family alive.
  • Whose jealousy knew no bound. They could strip a woman in public.
  • Refuse to honor their end of exile term.

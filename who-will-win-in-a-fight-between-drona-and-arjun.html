Who will win in a fight between Drona and Arjun?

📅 Nov 04, 2020 11:32 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

This is a simple question to answer. It's Arjuna. Every time. And there is no projection here. It was proved times and again.

Virat war

Arjuna defeated Drona along with other generals.

Kurukshetra

  1. After assuming the post of General Drona didn't dare to form Chakravyuh in Arjuna's presence for first two days.
  2. He couldn't defend Jaidradh on day 14 from Arjuna's wrath. Arjuna didn't wait for days hoping to someone to whisk Drona far away before vowing to kill Jaidradh.

But the point is Drona himself knew this and expressed on multiple occasions.

How could Arjuna be better than his Guru?

  1. Drona himself answered this question. What made Arjuna better than any warrior of his era was —
    1. He never stopped learning. Drona wasn't his only guru. He wasn't his last. He learnt a lot in Indralok and from Chitrasena. Shiva himself taught him.
    2. He was as generous in sharing his knowledge as he was greedy in acquiring them. He became guru as well.
  2. Drona, no doubt was a great teacher, but he was an academician. He didn't really had a field experience like Arjuna or Parshuram. We see him fight for the first time in Virata war. On other hand Arjuna's life is a series of wars, each making him stronger than before.

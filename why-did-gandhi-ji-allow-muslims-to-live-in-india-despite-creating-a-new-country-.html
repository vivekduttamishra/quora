Why did Gandhi Ji allow Muslims to live in India despite creating a new country for them?

📅 Jul 09, 2022 12:07 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Few reasons —

  1. Gandhiji wasn’t the King of India to either allow or deny anyone a living in India.
  2. Gandhiji wasn’t an official signatory on any agreement between Britain and Indian representatives.
  3. Migration of the population wasn’t part of the agreement that was signed. It was simply designed that portions of India with Muslim dominant population would become a separate nation.
  4. The partition caused immense loss of life and property on the two boundaries where India was partitioned. Now only an unimaginative mind may endorse movement of entire population at the risk of killing several more millions of people as the cross cities and states of India, furious at losing their home and expressing their grieves violently.

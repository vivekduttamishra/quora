What is Pandava's opinion about Karna on seeing his goodness though he was on the Adharma side and his skills which are much better than Arjuna?

📅 Apr 27, 2021 08:49 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Interesting question.

Which goodness are we referring to?

  • One in which Karna conspires multiple times to kill Pandavas?
  • One in which Karna cheats Parshurama?
  • One in which he sides with King in an attempt to throw Draupadi to fire because he failed Swyamvara?
  • One in which he asks Duhsasana to molest Draupadi?
  • One in which he persuades Duryodhana to go and mock Pandava’s in exile?

Seriously guys, which goodness of Karna are we talking about here?

Now let us turn to his skills.

WHAT SKILLS OF KARNA WERE SUPERIOR TO ARJUNA?

Now some may say that Arjuna was superior to Karna in everything because Karna never won any war against Arjuna. Ever. And while this is true, Karna still had few skills where Arjuna could never compete him —

  • No body could ever flee a battle field better than Karna — Forget Arjuna, no one managed to demonstrate this skill better than Karna did. Be it Druapadi’s swyamvara battle, Gandharva battle, Matasya battle or Kurukshetra battle, Karna holds the record for fleeing the largest number of time. Arjuna couldn’t do it even once. Even Abhimanyu failed to flee the battle — the fool didn’t know how to. Karna didn’t even teach this skill to his best friend Duryodhana, who too stayed put in Gandharva battle.
  • A great conspirator — Karna had been a career conspirator from as long as Arjuna was an archer. He was involved in every backstabbing acts of Duryodhana. Not only that there are acts where Karna is not a silent partner but chief instigator — Draupadi’s humilation, Ghosha Yatra, Matasya attack.
  • Saving your own hide — Karna knew how to save himself without caring about even his closest people. Not only he fled Matasya battle leaving Duryodhana behind, he also fled as Arjuna and Abhimanyu killed his brothers and sons.

But I don’t think any Pandava would have cherished any of these great acts.

Now coming to the question —

WHAT WAS PANDAVA’S VIEW ABOUT KARNA?

Still their indivicual opinion can be summed in his way

  • Yudhishthir — Had an exceptional and unreasonable high opinion and fear of Karna’s battle skill. Karna never gave Yudhishthira a reason to have such high opinions. But nevertheless, Yudhishthira remained worried from Karna’s angle. To be fair, Yudhishthir was never regarded as a military expert to be considered seriously against the likes of Parshurama, Krishna, or Bhishma’s opinion.
  • Bhima — Always hated Karna for who he was. In Bheema’s favour most the the conspiracies of Duryodhana were directed at Bheema personally and Karna was part of them. Naturally Bhima hated him. That hate grew multi fold when he tried to humilate Drauapdi. It was Bheema who announced that Arjuna would kill Karna. He didn’t show any remorse even when the true relationship was revealed following his death.
  • Arjuna — He never really cared about Karna till the day Draupadi was humilated. He let Karna walk away from swyamvara battle even though he could have killed him. Post Draupadi’s humilation, Arjuna knew he would kill him. And that was all Arjuna cared.
  • The Twins — They never seem to have any strong opinion about Karna. And after Draupadi’s humilation, there was no reason that they would have any high opinion about such an abuser.

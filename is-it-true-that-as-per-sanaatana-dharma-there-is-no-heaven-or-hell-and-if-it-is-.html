Is it true that as per Sanaatana Dharma, there is no heaven or hell? And if it is, why do we read that the Pandavas did not go to heaven?

📅 Sep 14, 2021 08:32 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Let us tackle the question in two parts.

1.Sanatana Dharma, heaven and hell.

Sanaatana Dharma did have the concept of heaven and hell, however, they are much different from western of biblical concept of heaven and hell.

First, unlike biblical theory, we don’t have the concept of a saitan being preceding deity of hell and having influence over human behavior in the same way as God does.

We realized that nothing can be parallel to one omnipresent God. He alone governs existence and beyond. There is no saitan who is equal to him or can influence someone beyond his wishes.

Some may find it amazing and may ask —

How, then, humans commit sins? How does God permit them to be sinners?

Answer is simple. God doesn’t interfere in human behavior. He doesn’t go on preventing a road accident or stopping someone from committing mistake. We are guided by our gunas — Sato, Rajas and Tamas. These gunas control our actions and actions our destiny — hence the heaven or hell.

Another important demarcation is —

The concept of the soul and the afterlife.

The soul is eternal, pure, unbreakable. But when it comes in contact with body, mind and desires, they caste a cloud on that eternal soul and soul undergoes a purification after the death. Life in heaven or hell is for a period in line with our actions. And once we clear our debts we are reborn. The soul continues it journey till it is freed from those bondages.

That means even the greatest of souls would suffer torment during his life and both heaven and hell after that.

Think, didn’t Rama suffered his father’s death or his beloved wife’s kidnapping? Didn’t Krishna suffered the destruction of his clan?

2.Why do we read Pandava’s didn’t go to heaven

The only possible reason is, you didn’t read it through and just followed the propaganda created by some fanatic. Pandavas stayed in hell for a very very short span of time after which they reached heaven. And this is in line with the concept of heaven and hell as per Santana philosophy. You must clear all your debts and you get all the credits.

Hope that clarifies your question.

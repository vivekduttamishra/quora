How do we still know years old Mahabharat? What preserved its story?

📅 May 13, 2020 07:34 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

This is a great question — how we preserved the story. It is almost impossible to preserve any writing or material for 5000 years or old. I am discussing this line in this series — Vivek Dutta Mishra (विवेक दत्त मिश्रा)'s post in The Accursed God.

What we did was to create a human chain spanning thousands of years and each generation passing the great knowledge to the next generation verbally. This the shruti aspect of our anicent knowledge. You may refer to my podcast detailing this story — भारत के संसृतिक विरासत की कहानी भाग १ --- क्या हैं श्रुति, स्मृति, वेद, उपनिषद, पुराण, महग्रन्थ, शास्त्र इत्यादि? by Storian Hindi • A podcast on Anchor

The human chain ensured that the story and more important, its lesson survive across time and this aboriginal method ensured that it not just survived but flourished over all those years.

Who was more patriot, Mahatma Gandhi or Nathuram Godse?

📅 Mar 14, 2020 09:06 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Who shines brightly — Sun or a piece of rock?

He is a greater patriot —

Who, the nation calls Bapu?

Who maintained the peace in Calcutta in August 1947?

Whom did the average Indian of 1921–1948 supported?

Who went to jail several times for Indian freedom movement?

Who worked for the farmers of Champaran?

Who championed for cleanliness?

Whom the entire world admires for his peace and nonviolence?

Who was not just a politician but a Mahatma — a social reformer?

Who united the entire India in a single freedom movement?

Whose influence extends over continent?

Who, when died, was mourned by the majority of Indians who performed rituals on his death the same way you do for your family member?

He can’t be called a patriot (forget great or small) who —

Was a mindless murderer filled with malice.

Had no verifiable contribution (unless you create one) in any form for India’s freedom struggle.

Had no support for anyone (other than a band of criminals)

Had no capability to prove Gandhi bad in front of masses.

Was no better than Kasabs or Afzal Gurus of this world who who kill someone because they can’t agree.

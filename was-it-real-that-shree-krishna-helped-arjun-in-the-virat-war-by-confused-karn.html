Thanks for the A2A. There is zero reference of Krishna’s involvement in Virata war in any known version of epic. Arjuna defeated everyone fair and square, and it wasn’t even surprising to anyone of that era. Both Kripacharya and Ashwatthama had warned about the possibility of a defeat at the hands of Arjuna.

Karna was alone to think he had a winning chance against Arjuna. In fact he felt so convinced about his victory that he was prepared to kill Arjuna and cut off his flag bearing the celestial ape.

Now you may ask, why would Krishna would need to help Arjuna? Did he help Arjuna during Draupadi’s swyamavara where Arjuna alone scared away Karna? Wasn’t he aware of Arjuna’s varlour in Indraloka and Gandharva war? Didn’t he knew the result of Gandharva war?

At a later stage this incident was requoted by Bhishma and Drona and no involvment of Krishna was ever discussed.

I can’t provide any reference, as there exist none.

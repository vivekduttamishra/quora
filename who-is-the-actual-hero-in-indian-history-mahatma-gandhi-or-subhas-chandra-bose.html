Who is the actual hero in Indian history, Mahatma Gandhi or Subhas Chandra Bose?

📅 Feb 25, 2020 11:47 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Both.

And any other answer would be grossly biased.

Both Mahatma Gandhi and Netaji Bose have rendered invaluable service during the freedom movement and despite the fact that they had difference of opinion doesn't make once contribution or dedication any less. It was Bose who first called Gandhiji as Bapu and supported quit India movement from outside India.

It is a modern social network trait to condemn one and glorify other — let us not fall into the trap and insult the great sons of our motherland.

Just for the information both these great leaders have been sketched in our original version of the constitution.

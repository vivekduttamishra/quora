Did Bhishma die before Karna?

📅 Mar 15, 2020 05:16 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Bhisma remained the commander in chief of Kaurava's army for first 10 days of the battle. On 10th day he was badly injured and pinned to ground by a mesh of arrows. (I won't go in this story in detail here). That was the last day of Bhisma in the war. But he didn't die — he had been blessed by his father of इक्षा मृत्यु — He can choose the time of his death. He decided to wait for the war to be over and wanted to see Hastinapur in able hands.

11th day Karna entered battle had he had decided not to fight under Bhisma's command (another answer wrongly mentioned Bhisma had barred him from fighting).

16th day after the death of Drona, the previous day Karna became the Supreme commander and was killed on the 17th day.

18th day the war was over.

Bhisma continued to live for more time — waited for Yudhisthir's coronation, and gave him a surmon on Rajyadharm. He waited for Sun to be in right astronomical position and died after 58 nights.

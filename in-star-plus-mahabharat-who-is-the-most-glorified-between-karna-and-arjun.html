In Star Plus Mahabharat, who is the most glorified between Karna and Arjun?

📅 Sep 16, 2020 09:23 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Hi,

Thanks for the A2A. It is not a question of who is more glorified. Arjuna has been de-glorified in Star Plus Mahabharata greatly —

Arjuna de-glorified.

  1. Let us start with the Arjuna title song — He got all happiness and glory. Glory, True. But happiness? He who spent most of his life in exile and struggle — lakshagrih, humilation of Draupadi, exile after dice game.
  2. It censured few important wars of Arjuna which could have shown his greatness, and both of them are well documented in all authentic versions of Mahabharata —
    1. The war that followed Swyamvara where Arjuna forced Karna to retreat. BRC showed a boil down version of this episode.
    2. Gandharva war, where Arjuna forced Gandharvas to release Duryodhana who was in his captivity. BRC showed boil down version of this episode.
    3. Arjuna’s fights on behalf of Indra against demons. To be fair with Star Plus, no TV show ever showed this war.
  3. It showed completely skewed narration of Matasya war — A war in which Arjuna single handedly defeated entire Kuru Army forcing the warriors to retreat multiple times and finally rendering all of the unconcious. Start Plus, made both Bhisma and Karna more powerful in this war than what happened in that war. BRC has shown this war in a fair detail.
  4. Like most of the other shows, it shows Arjuna killed Karna while he was unarmed, which contradicts original Mahabharata where Karna dies fighting. It is an injustice to both warriors, Karna and Arjuna.

Arjuna glorified

It shows Arjuna felt sorry for Karna when Indra traded Karna’s Kavacha. In actaul text, Arjuna and other Pandvas were pleased to learn Karna lost his Kavacha. To be fair, even Karna was pleased at the exchange.

Story altered

It shows Arjuna had an appreciation for Karna which is not true. For most of the time Arjuna was indiffererent towards Karna, and had a natural disliking for Karna for being an ally of Duryodhana. He had a complete contempt against Karna after Draupadi’s humilation.

I will not cover Karna’s part in my story as I don’t want this answer to become a battle ground for Karna-Arjuna camps.

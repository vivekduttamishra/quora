Who should be respected more among Karna and Arjuna?

📅 Mar 26, 2020 12:32 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Well depends on weather you value epic or fable, weather you value morality or ambition, Krishna or Duryodhana.

In the epic called Mahabharat, Karna in absolute sense represents an evil who —

  • Is deceitful to his guru
  • Is Foul-mouthed not only for Draupadi, but to his guru Drona, commander Bhisma
  • Pokes Dussasan to strip Draupadi naked
  • Provokes Duryodhana to go and make fun of Pandavas in exile.
  • Who boasts more and performs less.
  • He isn't as faithful to his friend as fables show. Duryodhan is a greater friend.
  • Is rebuked by Ashwathama, Bhisma, Drona at different point because of his arrogance, boasting and foul mouthed nature.

Arjun on the other hand has his share of weakness —

  • He follows his brother even when he shouldn't have.

There were qualities that made him respectable

  • He was hardworking, focused, and favorite of Guru.
  • He was adored by not only Bhisma and Krishna but even by enemy (Drupad),
  • He earned the blessings of Mahabharat.
  • He alone questioned the need of war giving humanity the blessings of Gita.

Now fabbles to have a story that makes Karna a victim, powerful and generous —

  • Karna cheats his guru because he had to… Parshuram shouldn't have cursed him
  • Drona refused him education — false story.
  • Draupadi rejected him — controversial. False or at least over hyped.
  • His worst mistake, he called Draupadi a whore as a repayment of insult she did in Swaymber. Those fable censor the fact that Karna actually commanded Dussasan to strip Draupadi. There was nothing respectable about Karna!
  • Fables tell Bhisma forbid Karna to fight under his command — fake! Karna refused to fight under Bhisma's command.

There is not comparison who is more…one is and one isn't.

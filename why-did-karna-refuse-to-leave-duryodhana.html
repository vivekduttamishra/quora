Why did Karna refuse to leave Duryodhana?

📅 Mar 14, 2020 10:10 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

There are two important point to consider —

Why Karna refused to leave Duryodhana?

  1. What options he had? He was made a king by Duryodhana and leaving him, he would have to leave his kingship.
  2. Karna’s main goal of life was to fight Arjuna to prove himself better — how would he get that opportunity by agreeing to Krishna or Kunti?

Did Karna really no leave Duryodhana?

  1. What happened in Gandharvas conflict? Did Karna stood up and fight when Duryodhana was captured?
  2. Didn’t he ran away twice in Virata war?
  3. Why didn’t he participate in first 10 days of Mahabharat battle?

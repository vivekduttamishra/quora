Why did Netaji choose to hide if alive after 1945?

📅 Mar 18, 2020 05:05 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Netaji wasn't alive after 1945 — He definitely died in the crash —

  • He was too great a personality to hide in shadows and live or die as some Gumnami. He was a bright sun and no mist or cloud can hide him for a long time. No prison could contain him.
  • He was too great a general to hide for the fear of any prosecution and leave his Indian army to be taken a war prisoner.
  • Britishers would have announced his arrest as a grand prize had they succeeded, but they failed.
  • Only fate could have stopped Bose and his army. He who knew how to make his way through impossible obstacles.

I know without doubt only death could have stopped this grand man.

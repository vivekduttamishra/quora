Why do we all hate the slayer of Karna? Is it because Arjuna did not show any mercy for Karna in the final battle?

📅 Mar 18, 2020 07:53 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Another leading question that leads no where.

We don't hate the slayer of Karna — not everyone. The world is divided into two groups — those who have read Mahabharat and those who love Karna.

Those who have read Mahabharat don't hate the slayer of Karna because they know —

  • Karna died in a fair battle without chariot but not unarmed.
  • Karna just didn't sided with evil but acted evil and was punished for his misdeeds.
  • Lord Krishna himself endorsed and commanded Arjun to slay him. He didn't hate Arjuna.
  • Because unlike Karna Arjuna never yearned for fight — he was the one to question the relevance of war.

The other group who never read the Mahabharata the hate because —

  • They think Karna as a superior warrior who could have mopped Arjun — they don't know Arjuna proved himself superior on every single occasion — Draupadi Swaymber, Gandharva battle and Virat battle.
  • They have absolutely no idea Karna was badly bitten not only by Arjuna but Bhim, Abhimanyu and Satyaki and don't forget Ghatotkatch.
  • They don't know Karna commanded Dussasan to strip Draupadi — or they feel because Draupadi rejected Karna he was entitled to strip her in public. They also don't know other story of Draupadi Swaymber which is not relevant here.

And there are many such things that TV shows made them believe in the goodness and powers of Karna so much that they started question even Krishna.

Why did Krishna not react or punish Draupadi when she insulted Karna during Swayamvaram? Even the Sun reacted but was not punished. She stopped the anger of the Sun by saying unrelated bla bla.

📅 May 23, 2020 01:26 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

To understand the true answer to the question let us rephrase — Krishna didn't react or punish Draupadi. Why? There are two possibilities —

  1. She did nothing wrong.
  2. Krishna wasn't aware of her doing anything wrong.

And in this case both answers apply. Krishna wasn't aware of her doing anything wrong because she didn't insult him. This is also verifiable not only in Swaymber parva of Bori critical edition, but also Neelkantha hindi sanskrit version, Dakshinayat. Further Draupadi insulting Karna is not once mentioned anywhere beyond Swaymber.

Now this is a new mock — Draupadi subdued Surya, the father of Karna by some bla bla. Was he stupid? Or someone misunderstood the context?

Arjuna fans like Aada Singh say that Krishna-karna meeting was just a fake story, created by Sanjaya because Sanjaya did not have divine power at that time then how he narrated this meeting, did Sanjaya really not possesses divine sight at that time?

📅 Aug 10, 2020 01:10 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

I don't see any so called Arjuna fan to doubt the Karna Krishna meeting, though some may have their personal opinion.

As a follower of the epic, I believe in the incident, I believe Sanjay and Vaisampanya Das and Ved Vyas.

This incident is maintained in BORI, and long before BORI, it was maintained by the Vaisampanyadas. Had it been wrongly narrated by Sanjay, he could have removed it or Ved Vyas could have removed it.

As far as the story sequence goes there can be few explanations —

  • Most logical, Mahabharat story doesn't progress in sequence
  • Sanjay heard the story from other sources.
  • Sanjay knew the past and present due to divine vision.
  • Sanjay was the one to tell the story to Dhritarashtra

Either way, the story stands as Vyas story and not an interpolation.

Hi,

BORI is not an interpolation. It’s critical. And it’s not exact. In fact, there is no single script that can be considered as an exact. There are over a 1000 manuscript dating back over 500 years. And the event on which the text were compiled in over 5000 years. So variation in the narrative of a real fact was natural.

We have different narratives to incidents that are currently transpiring — who won India-Pakistan war in 1965? How did Sushant Singh Rajput die?

And we are talking about a 5000 years old incident narrated verbally for thousands of years in different language and culture. So while major elements of the narrative validates the facts, their are minor variations.

Why is BORI referred.

BORI is considered more exact than anyone else because —

  • BORI stuied more than 1259 manuscript spanning different language and region including Nepali, Kasmiri, Maithili, Bengali, Tamil, Malyalam apart from Sanskrit editions.
  • The research spanned over 4 decades of careful study of all those manuscript removing inconsistencies and interpolations. They employeed following techniques.

How BORI removed interpolations

  1. They removed all those incidents which occured in very few scripts and were inconsistent with the rest of the narratives. Let us take an examples.
    1. Draupadi stopped Karna in her swyamvara — This incident occured in just 4 out of 1259 Manuscripts including Nepali manuscript N2, which is one of the later editions. (People often confuse with N3, which is one of the oldest and doesn’t include this interpolation). The narrative is present in one of the most famous — Neelkantha recitation, which also includes its contradiction. Now a translation like that of Kisari Mohan Ganguli which is predominently based on Neelkantha would include this narrative, but BORI after comparing all the manuscript dropped it. This makes BORI version more authentic than KMG.
  2. Cross Referencing technqiue For an incident to be exact, it is most likely cross referred at multiple other places in epic — way when one person talks to other about the incident. Such reference along with its existence in multiple manuscript authenticate the incident.
    1. Drauapdi Cheerharan — The incident finds a mentions at several places. Her molestation is constantly referred at multiple places, including Virata Parva, Vana Parva, Udyoga Parva, after Duyodhana’s vadh etc.
    2. Gandharva War — It has been cross referred at multiple places like Virata Parva, Udoyoga Parva, Ghatotkatch vadha Parva and so on.
    3. Virata War — Itself is referred in Udyoga Parva, Ghatotkatch vadha Parva and so on.

Where BORI is not exact?

At a couple of places BORI overstepped its repsonibility and modified the script because they thought it may not be acceptable to contemporary audience. There are just a few cases, but their they deviate from all the sources they had —

  • Krishna’s role as protector of Draupadi in Sabha Parva — Most manuscript in both nothern and southern recitation include Krishna role as protector of Draupadi. However the exact way in which he protected differs. So BORI subsitituted Dharma for Krishna. Many take it as an excuse to say entire Sabha Parva is interpolation. However, BORI included the rest of the incidents including Dussassana attempt to disrobe her and Karna’s role as the instigator.
  • Jaidradh Vadha Parva — BORI removed Krishna’s role in creating illusion of sun set to Kill Jaidradh. Again various narratives differed in detailing of this event and that made them BORI eliminate the details.

But such incidents is too few to condemn BORI and therefore people keep referring it for a justified reason which can’t be undone by few critics here on Quora.

Has Ekta Kapoor really destroyed the Mahabharata?

📅 Mar 14, 2021 09:52 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Did Ekta Kapoor distort the Mahabharata narrative?

Definitely.

Was her presentation poor?

No. It was horrible.

Did she destroy Mahabharata?

NO. She may have. But she didn't. Her serial didn't run long enough to do any real damage. It failed to impress any generation. It was never a popular show and never completed.

The true award to distort the great epic should go to Mr Siddharth Kumar Tiwari and his retelling and series like Suryaputra Karna. These series have worst degree of interpolations and distortions, and unfortunately, unlike Ekta Kapoor, series they completed even reruns. These series also got opportunity to actually influence the mindset of the new generation. Many belive those craps.

And that is what you should call destroying the epic — real damage.

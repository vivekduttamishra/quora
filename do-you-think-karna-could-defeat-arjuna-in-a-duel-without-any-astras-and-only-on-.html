There is not one instance mentioned in entire Mahabharat where Karna defeated Arjun.

In Swaymber Karna attacked Arjun but retreated after seeing his battle. There were no special weapons.

The key difference between Karna and Arjun —

  • Arjun was focused as a warrior while Karna's ambition clouded his judgements.
  • Arjun fought against Gandharvas on the order of his commander (Yudhisthir) Karna constantly mocked his guru (drona) and commander (Bhisma)
  • Arjun never ran from a war, Karna did on multiple occasions. That indicates his lack of confidence and focus.
  • Arjun inspired (Satyaki and Prince Uttar of Virat. He became a guru). Karna boasted.

None of the points mentioned above are about special weapon but the core character that defines or defies any one including an archer

Why did Kunti lie to her husband about Karna?

📅 Mar 17, 2020 05:48 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Kunti didn't lie to Pandu — she didn't disclosed about her. Although she knew about the whereabouts of Karna as per ancient scripts, she didn't disclose his identity. she may have been hasitent.

Why was she hesitant needs no explanation in contemporary Era, but why was she hesitant in Mahabharat Era that was lot liberal sometimes baffles me — it was acceptable as Krishna used reference of shastras to announce him a Pandu Putra later.

But there are other stories in Mahabharat where a lady suppressed the identity of her child born outside marriage. Satyawati too didn't disclose the identity of Vyas to his husband but only after his death — may be as per shastras husbands were obliged to accept wife's son from before marriage but certainly not obliged to marry her in the first place.

Why unlike Satyawati, why she remained hesitant to disclose Kara's identity, when she later met the youth Karna is not explained but can be guessed — her hesitation let Karna slip away out of Pandava's camp (not that he was ever in)

That brings us back to square one — I guess human psychology is too complicated to have an objective answer.

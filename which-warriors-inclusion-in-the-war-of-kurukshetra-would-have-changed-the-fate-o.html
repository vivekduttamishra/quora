📅 Sep 30, 2020 08:28 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

This question has one absolute answer. That warrior was Vivek — Oh! Wait. Not me. I mean wisdom.

Had Kaurava's followed wisdom to know where to stop, and whom to listen. Had they realized and ammneded their ways as Krishna walked into the court of Hastinapur…

No one else — neither Ravana, nor Meghnad nor any one else could have changed the destiny of Kauravas which were guided by destiny and willed by Krishna.

Kauravas had no dirth of fighters. Mighty Bhishma, who roared on Pandavas for 10 days making their victory impossible. Ashwathama, Drona, Shalya and even Karna were great warriors. What else they had in common? They sided with Adharma and against Lord Krishna.

Which warrior could have changed faith of Kaurava's? Krishna had no such attachment to his vow that he won't break. Didn't he broke his vow against Bhishma? Who could have joined Kauravas to change their fate? And why wouid any follower of Dharma support them?

Guru Dronacharya said that Arjun was the best archer in the world but how come there was no other archer in the world who could deafet Arjun other than karna, bhisma, eklavya. Was there no other archer in the globe or in other foreign countries?

📅 May 15, 2020 05:52 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Great question. Little provocative. But works. Let attack the question layer by layer —

What does best in the world mean?

  • Have you ever heard about Miss Universe pageant? Is the winner really the best in Universe? I mean, whole of universe? Are we sure we accounted for all lives in all the speicies in all the planets across all the galaxies?
  • When Drona referred Arjuna as the best archer of the world, he was a teacher and he judged Arjuna based on —
    • His experience with archery and understanding of its technique.
    • His experience with Arjuna and other students of Archery and their comparison.
    • His knowledge of Archery, history of archery and Arjuna’s comparative performance gave him that confidence.
  • When he said ‘Best in the world’, he meant best in the known and civilized world and he didn’t set on a journey to discover all the warriors around those parts of the world where civilization was unheard of.

What about the list of exceptional warriors mentioned as a part of the question?

  • Important point to note is, even if one warrior bests Arjuna, it is proved beyond doubt that he isn’t the best. So if there are three warrior in the list, no matter what Drona says he can’t be considered best. But did any of them actually and ever defeated Arjuna?
  • Eklavya — There never had been any battle between Arjuna and Eklavya either directly or indirectly. There is not mention of Ekalavya ever defeating other warriors like Bhisma, Drona, Ashwatthama, Karna or anyone to prove his worthyness even to list in this question. He was killed by Krishna.
  • Karna — There were at least two indirect and two direct battle between Karna and Arjuna before their faceoff at Kurukshetra, and I am trying to remember in which of them Arjuna was defeated —
    • Indirect Battles
      • Guru Dakshina War Against Drupad — One of the two failed and other succeeded. Arjuna imprisoned Drupad.
      • Gandharva War — Again one of them failed and fled, other fought and vanquished the same enemy. Arjuna finally rescued Duryodhana from Gandharvas.
    • Direct Battles
      • Draupadi Swyamber — One of the retreated by naming other as personification of art of war comparable to Indira, and Ram. Arjuna let him go.
      • Virat War — The two confornted and one of them retreated twice. Arjuna single handedly bested entire Kauravas army. This was exactly what Bhishma and Kripacharya had predicted.
  • Bhishma — Arjuna and Bhishma fought against each other only once before the battle of Kurukshetra. The Virata war, where Arjuna defeated Bhisma. In the battle of Kurukshetra, again Arjuna defeated Bhishma.

What about other warriors?

  • Arjuna fought a lot of battle across his life and several included war beyond this world. Is that counted in other parts of the world? And Arjuna proved the words of his guru.

Is Karna capable of defeating Indra?

📅 Mar 20, 2021 12:29 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

Short Answer: NO.

Unlike the popular portrayal of Indra in Indian TV Shows, in Mahabharata Indra, is the epitome of power and often used as metaphor for the power. When someone fights with extreme valour, he is referred as like Indra.

There is no sequence where Karna ever had time to fight Indra. But Karna has lost too many battles by lesser powerful warriors to have any hopes of competing with Indra. Let us see some important references to prove why Karna would cannot defeat Indra —

  • Karna was badly defeated and forced to flee Gandharvas who merely worked under the command of Indra. Without defeating them, Karna had no hope of reaching Indra.
  • After Draupadi’s Swayamvara, when Karna found himself losing to Arjuna (in disguise) he compares him with Indra, considers his invincible and retreates scared.
  • Karna demands Vasvi Shakti in exchange of his Kavacha, and it was one of his most prized possession till the time he reluctantly used it on Ghatotkatcha. It was one time use only for Karna. And it wasn’t the most powerful weapon of Indra, that would be Vajra. Now imagine Indra with Vajra and unlimited use of Vasvi Shakti against Karna, who couldn’t win against Abhimanyu and lost several battles to Bhima or Satyaki (although he defeated them as well)

Looking at a larger canvas, it was extremely unlikely for Karna to have given any competition to Indra, forget winning against him.

Short Answer: YES.

Lord Shiva willed Pandava’s to win. Krishna sided with them. And both proclaimed in no unclear term that Pandava’s would win the war! There was nothing that could have changed the outcome.

Imagine the power of Dharma — It makes a young warrior Abhimanyu more powerful than the entire Kaurava troop and a Rakshasha creating havoc in army full of warriors with Divyastra.

Abhimanyu existed to prove wickedness may make most powerful warrior like Arjuna indisposed for a moment, but can’t control the destiny, which would find a way to exert himself — If not Arjuna, then Abhimanyu; If not Abhimanyu then someone else.

Ghatotkatch too exist to prove never to underestimate enemies. You may think you can out perform by fighting a war at night! But then you can’t control the destiny. Abhimanyu may be dead. But then some Ghatotkatch would rise to the occasion and throw away your best laid plans.

Those with deceit in the mind can’t control the destiny. They may commandeer a large army but they are destined to lose.

If Karna fans and Arjun fans fight each other, who will win?

📅 Apr 10, 2021 03:18 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

This is an interesting question. Who would win the war. I would say it depends on which weapons are going to be used in this war. Let us see what arsenal each team has got.

WEAPONS OF ARJUNA’S FAN

The authentic narrative of Ved Vyas which is over five thousand years old. It includes various written scriptures dating back several centuries written in different languages.

If we follow this narrative Arjuna and Arjuna fan’s are going to win every single round. Because in this epic not only Krishna, Hanuman and Shiva would support Arjuna but every auspicious beings of the world would support Arjuna. Every great power, every positive energy would support Arjuna. Karna would be supported by negative elements like demons, smaller snakes and crows.

Arjuna’s fan put their faith in Krishna, Shiva and authentic text, where the Gods of all the world announced without biased or doubt that Arjuna would win everytime.

WEAPONS OF KARNA’S FANS

Against Arjuna’s weapon, there are several potent weapons in the hand of Karna’s fans —

  1. Fake narrataives by novels and TVShows. There are a number of TVShows and fake narratives that potrays Karna as a much better person and warrior than he was. Either his failures are censured or glorified or justified with equally baseless narratives. Such shows and light reading are often more popular references for common people and are easily swayed into believing them. And in those narratives, Karna is decidedly stands a chance to win. With those as their weapon, Karna’s fans have a winning chance by seer popularity and ignorance.
  2. Selective misquoted references from authentic epic. Mahabharata, being an unbiased narrative, also talks about a few good qualities that Karna had. Even Krishna spoke about them. And that gives them the choice to ignore the facts and focus on few statements, which Krishna has often said more for psychological effects than for facts. Many of those statements are so contradictory to the fact that anyone would see the reality except those who really want to see. Then they won’t accept all of Krishna’s observations. Just a few of them, that suits them.
  3. THE MOST POTENT WEAPON KARNA’S FAN HAVE — The ultimate weapon that Karna’s fans have is a weapon that can neutralize any attack of Arjuna’s fan. It is to declare any and every part of Vyas Mahabharata as interpolation where Karna has been shown in a bad light, which essentially means over 90% of the epic. So Vastraharan, Gandahrva battle, Virata Battle, Swyamvara battle all becomes interpolations. That is an excellent strategy. If you can’t win a debate declare the references invalid.
  4. Dopplegangers — It appears that there are several doppelgangers of Karna in the epic. They are his namesakes and they are always eager to take the blame of every Karna’s failure as some other Karna failed although the epic makes no such reference in the context. So in entire Draupadi’s swyamvara parva only a single Karna is mentioned and scholars like Vishnu Suthankar confirms that he was the Karna that participated in Swyamvara. Same is also present in Neelkantha and other source references. But for them he was some other Karna who failed at Swyamvara and then suddenly vanished. They also invented another Anga, and interestingly a king ruling that Anga at the same time also known as Karna. He gets defeated by Bhima during Rajsuy yagna instead of the great Karna and then he too vanishes with no further traces.

With so many arsenal and with so many people following those references against so few following the authentic epic, in a fan war Karna’s fans have got much better chance at winning than Karna ever had.

They may win. They can’t change the past. They can’t change the facts. They can’t change the eternal sanatana philosophy — Dharmo Rakshati Rakshitaah!

So who do you think, would win?

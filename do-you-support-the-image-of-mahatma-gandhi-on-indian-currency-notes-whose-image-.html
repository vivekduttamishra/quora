Do you support the image of Mahatma Gandhi on Indian currency notes? Whose image do you want to see on the Indian currency?

📅 Mar 04, 2022 08:16 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Short Answer: Yes, I support image of Mahatma Gandhi on Indian currency notes.

At the onset, I must make it clear that India have millions of patriots, lacks of martyrs, thousands of leaders, hundreds of legends and they all deserve our highest respect for their contribution towards our nation, toward each of our individual lives and he who doesn’t respect them is ungrateful. I respect all our great Indian leaders from Maharana Pratap to Chandra Shekhar Azad, from Bhagat Singh to Shubhash Chandra Bose.

We have thousands of statues, stamps, institutions, places named after these great men and it was rightly said —

साहिदों की चिताओं पर जुड़ेंगे हर वर्ष मेले,
वतन में मिटने वालों का यही आखरी निशां होगा।

These great men didn’t die for the fame of face on the currency. They died for the country and we must not belittle their contribution by pitching them against each other in the race of whose face should be on the currency.

That leads to the key question —

WHY ONLY MAHATMA GANDHI ON CURRENCY?

But, we MUST understand currency of a country isn’t just an internal affair of that country. It is a way national identity. And the world doesn’t identify India with names of all our great martyrs and patriots.

Mahatma Gandhi was and even today is, undoubtedly not only the most recognized Indian, but most revered across the globe. Removing him from the currency doesn’t make him fade from the memories of all his admirers from the world, it would rob India of her greatest honor.

If you want to understand the impact of Gandhiji ask few simple questions —

  • Which other leader of India has one fourth of the number of busts erected in different countries of the world?
  • How many Indian leaders other than Gandhiji gets a birthday celebration across the globe?
  • More than one hundred and fifty countries have issued stamps, coins in the honor of Mahatma Gandhi. Who is the next leader to have been bestowed a similar honor across the world?
  • People have been proudly known as — Black Gandhi, Russian Gandhi, Simant Gandhi. Is there any other name that is used across the globe with similar reverence?
  • Subhash Chandra Bose, the great freedom fighter called Mahatma Gandhi as the father of the nation. Was Bose a fool or a flatter to have called him father of the nation after being with him for over two decades. Or as I see was a visionary and a true patriot?
  • Father of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minn said — “I and others may be revolutionaries, but we are disciples of Mahatma Gandhi.” Do we have any other parallel?
  • Lord Mount Batten and Barak Obama kept Gandhi among the top influencers in their life.

But these are great men. Now to understand the impact of Mahatma Gandhi, I would ask you to turn towards popular USA TV Shows and you would commonly find ‘Gandhi’ used as an adjective for peace. And there are hundreds of them. Let me give you an example. You may check the time stamp and watch the episode of Amazon Prime to see how police officers are using Gandhiji’s name

And this is not an isolated case. I can quote dozen more.

The idea is Mahatma Gandhi is a legacy to be cherished not to be tarnished. Some day some government in their foolhardy may banish him from the currency note and may even pass a parliament bill that he is not our father of the nation. But in doing so, they would do dis-service to our own great nation and NOT to Mahatma Gandhi. He is beyond our disrespect. They won’t have power to banish the man whom the world hailed as the ‘greatest man of the century’ from their memory.

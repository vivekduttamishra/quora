Why should we support Mahatma Gandhi?

📅 Mar 06, 2021 12:56 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Don’t.

Mahatma Gandhi doesn’t need our support. His ideas sustain a sensible society. We get the honour just be associating us with the father of the nation.

‘Mahatma Gandhi’ is just the name of a political personality, or a freedom fighter or a reformer. Today it has become an adjective worldwide. Forget Indian, even global TVShows and cinema invokes his name as an adjective for the value he stood fo.

Whether it is a spiritual leader like Dalai Lama or technology innovator Steve Jobs or the prominent world leaders like Narendra Modi and Barack Obama, they all draw inspiration from that man whom generations to come shall scarce believe that such a one as this ever in flesh and blood walked upon this earth.

And we are here debating why should we support him, as if he is dying to get our support. The question is, do you have a better support system? If yes, please follow that path. Gandhiji would well survive, without the support of those who have even a flickering doubt if they should support him or not.

But before you decide what you should and shouldn’t be doing, please discover Gandhi through the eyes of a visionary world-wide and verifiable evidences, rather than social network and debate on WhatsApp, Facebook or Quora (including this post of mine).

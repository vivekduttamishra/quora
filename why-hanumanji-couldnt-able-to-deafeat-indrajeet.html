Why hanumanji couldn't able to deafeat indrajeet?

📅 Apr 15, 2020 10:20 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Hanumanji had a boon from Brahma that made him invincible as no weapon could ever harm him. Indrajeet didn't defeat him. He surrendered. Here are few reasons —

  1. He didn't want to insult the devine weapon thrown on him.
  2. He had entered lanka to locate Sita. But, wise beyond imagination that he was, he also used the opportunity to
    1. Checkout Lanka's defence and offence capacity.
    2. Checkout possible allies.
  3. He wanted to end his quest by meeting Raavan and showcasing his power to instill fear in them.
  4. It becomes very apparent later when he set fire to Lanka and returned unchallenged. Where was might Indrajeet at that time?

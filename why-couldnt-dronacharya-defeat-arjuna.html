Why couldn't Dronacharya defeat Arjuna?

📅 May 06, 2020 02:23 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

There are several reasons why Drona could never have defeated Arjuna —

  • Drona was an excellent teacher of warfare but almost no experience of real war. He was an academician. His first battle was Virat and then Kurukshetra. Arjuna was an experienced warrior with lot of victories to his credit including Rajsue Yagna Gandharva war, Swaymber battle etc.
  • Arjuna learnt his lesson not only from Drona, but was a lifetime learner.
  • Arjuna was the only warrior to be blessed by Lord Shiva.
  • The day Drona sided with Adharma, he stood not only against Arjuna but Krishna. He was Doomed.

My answer is based on the story of Mahabharat an Indian epic and refers to authentic versions including —

  • English translation by Kisari Mohan Ganguli (KMG),
  • Bibek Debroy translation of Bori critical edition and
  • Neelkanth edition Sanskrit Hindi version of Gita Press.
  • Mahabharat by Rajgopalachari based on Dakshinayat

Who is powerful comparatively, Udhisthira or Duryodhana?

📅 May 03, 2020 11:12 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for A2A.

Before answering the question, let us understand power is not just a physical trait but also the trait of the character. Who is more powerful depends on what power we are talking about? If it is about the physical strength or capability to use a mace or ruthlessness, Duryodhan was more powerful than Yudhisthir.

But as they were contestent for the throne, question is where did the real power lie. And in the following question lies the answer —

  • Who was the afraid one — Yudhisthir or Duryodhan?
  • Who conspired to kill the other — Yudhisthir or Duryodhan?
  • Who tried to protect other despite of later’s deceit in Gandharva episode — Yudhishthir or Duryodhan?
  • Who was capable for forgiving — Yudhisthir or Duryodhan?

Only a powerful person can forgive, only a coward conspires.

My answer is based on the story of Mahabharat, the Great Indian Epic. My answer refers to the —

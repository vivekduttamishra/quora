Who named gandhi as 'father of india'?

📅 Mar 18, 2020 08:50 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

While it may be debatable who called Gandhiji as the father of the nation in English, he was fondly referred as Bapu long before India became an independent nation and got its constitution. It is believed that Netaji Shubhash Chandra Bose first called Gandhiji as Bapu.

And so sure were the creators of the constitution that they put a picture in the original book where the word Father of the nation is mentioned for Gandhiji — its in the constitution after all.

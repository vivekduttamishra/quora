Why are Gandhi's principles and ideals not relevant in today's generation?

📅 Feb 26, 2020 01:29 AM PST  |  🌐 English

This is a leading question not trying to seek an unbiased rational answer — it is based on the premise that Gandhian ideals are not relevant. If you have this conviction probably you have an opinion (I won't call it answer), there is no point in raising the question!

So I would rather debate on why the question is biased and leading.

Gandhiji is not just a single focused agenda — his ideals are holistic and multidimensional. They were and they will remain relevant for all times to come.

Cleanliness

Gandhiji emphasized on cleanliness. Who can argue about its relevance? P.M modi followed this Gandhian idea beautifully.

Self discipline and self dependence

Gandhiji promoted the idea of everyone doing their part in a community leaving. He himself worked and promoted the idea of practice before you preach! Why do you think Gandhi dominated international (read last word 3 times) opinion for such a long time? His movement became people movement. He empowerd common people who in turn empowered him.

Swadeshi and cottage industry

Gandhi emphasized on decentralization (distribution) of industry as cottage industry. Before you frawn on the idea ask yourself what is whatsapp if not a product of cottage industry.

Wait probably by Gandhian thought you mean only NON VIOLENCE!

Let's analyse — was an armed struggle for independence possible?

  • How was someone supposed to form army of people already fighting hunger, famine and illiteracy? Where was the fund for weapon, uniform, food and salary? It was easy to train people to fast (poverty had made them expert) protest non violent in khadi.
  • I have utmost respect for Netaji Shubhas Chandra Bose. But his army could never reach India, forgot winning a war. The problem is it is easy to suppress a rebellion — all you need is a superior army. But l difficult to crush a hapless non violent movement because blemishing your name — Jakianwala bag remains a unforgivable scar on British rule. Crushing INA or hanging Bhagat Singh didn't make them apologize.

Wait we should be talking relevance in today's world.

  • Do you remember the success of Anna Hazare movement in recent years?
  • Do you also know the fate of LTTE and Saddam Hussain?
  • Do you know who considers his ideology relevant — Steve Jobs, Barrak Obama, Dalali Lama

Is your question relevant?

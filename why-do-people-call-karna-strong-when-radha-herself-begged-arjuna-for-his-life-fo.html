Why do people call Karna strong when Radha herself begged Arjuna for his life for the first 16 days according to BORI?

📅 Sep 06, 2020 08:43 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

These questions are actually SPAMS, no wonder always asked anonymously. I wish quora should remove this overly misused features.

This is spam, because the anonymous questioner hasn't read BORI, but trying to propagate his views in the name of BORI, thus trying to sound authentic, but in reality doing more damage to the great epic than any individual.

Karna, was on of the stronger fighters and renowned archer of his time. Proud as he was, it's illogical to say he begged for his life for first 16 days.

Infact, since he had refused to fight under Bhisma's command, he was absent from the war for first 10 days. Even in not fighting he was confident that once he enters the war, he would route Pandavas. While this confidence or claim was not accurate, this proves he didn't need to beg for his life. He could have simply refused to fight, which he didn't.

He fought actively, wrecking havoc on Pandavas army killing Ghatotkatch and Abhimanyu, although the killing of later was an embarrassing episode even for Karna.

He fought, won, lost, escaped an active fight, returned and fought again — he didn't really begged for his life.

In fact long before the battle he agreed to Krishna the battle for Kaurav wasn't winnable, yet, he fought — this shows he was brave.

On the last day of the of his life, he indeed asked Arjuna to wait till he can pull out his wheel stuck in the blood puddle. It is not relevant what alternatives he had, but he didn't beg, he demanded, and when his demand was sidelined he fought and died. Didn't beg for mercy as the questioner wants to make people believe.

📅 Apr 08, 2021 11:45 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

But questions like these don’t deserve an answer, they need to be reported. They are not trying to seek sincere answer; they are hate campaigns.

So who over-glorified Gandhiji more than the person who called him — The father of the nation? Yes, had Subhas Chandra Bose been alive, he would have been the first one to censure such a question. Do you think Netaji was a brainwashed youth, to have such a reverence for a coward?

You hate Mahatma Gandhi, that is understood from the tone of your question, but why insult Subhash Chandra Bose? Why insult Dr Rajendra Prasad who equated Gandhiji with Ganga? Was he too a brainwashed? Is PM Modi a fool to follow Gandhji’s ideal? Was Barak Obama a fool to call Gandhiji his inspiration?

Why do you say Brithishers left India for the fear of Subhash Chandra Bose or INA? Why not because of the fear of Rani Lakshmi Bai? Or because they were afraid of Maharana Pratap? All these great heros have one thing in common — they weren’t alive when Brithishers left. Bose had disappeared in 1945 and so had INA. So were Brithshers afraid of the ghost of Shubhash Chandra Bose?

Brithshers were indeed afraid of the spirit of heros like Shubhash Chandra Bose, Maharana Pratap, and Laksmi Bai that had manifested in Indian hearts. They sacrifice were burning and continue to burn in the hearts of millions of true Indians till eternity. But the guiding light that was leading India in those trying hours against all irrationality and senseless violence was that of Gandhiji.

To say Gandhiji is overhyped and had no contribution in India’s freedom is not just an insult of Gandhiji but crores of Indians who followed his footsteps and fought for Indias independence. Those Indians also included the likes of Chandrashekhar Azad and Shubhash Chandra Bose at certain point in time. Gandhian movement was not a private movement but national movement.

Unfortunately it has become a trend to critisize Indian heros, pit them against each other like a bull-fight. Unfortunately people don’t realize that they are actively digging the grave of they own glorious history. No matter who wins the debate, we would lose — some will condemn one hero to favour other and others will resiprocate.

Be careful. You won’t be able to turn Gandhji and Netaji against each other. You won’t be able to turn nation against them. You would only mock yourself and our national history on an international forum.

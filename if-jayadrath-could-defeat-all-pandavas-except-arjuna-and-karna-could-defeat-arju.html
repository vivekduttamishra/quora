If Jayadrath could defeat all Pandavas except Arjuna and Karna could defeat Arjuna in a fair fight, how did Pandavas manage to win?

📅 Nov 22, 2020 10:52 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Hi, thanks for the A2A.

The question makes too many assumptions, all baseless. Yet, it manages to answer itself. Just rephrase it — Pandavas won, despite of Jaidradh and Karna, what does that tell you about their calibre?

Not sure, let us ask few questions?

  1. If Karna was better than Arjuna, and Jaidradh better than the rest, why Kauravas waited for Arjuna to be away before forming a Chakravyuh? Karna could have defeated Arjuna and Jaidradh rest of them and war would have concluded on day 11 itself?
  2. If Karna was a better than Arjuna why didn't he kill Arjuna between day 11 and day 17. His chariot wasn't stuck in mud the rest of the days?
  3. If Karna was a better warrior, why couldn't he protect Jaidradh from Arjuna on day 14?
  4. If Karna was a better warrior why did he need Vashavi Shakti to kill Arjuna? Why did he tell Indra, he was scared of Arjuna? Karna surely lost his Kavach, but Arjuna never had such Kavach in the first place? Why did he not asked Vasvi Shakti from his father? Why did he not used Pashupatastra on Karna? Why didn't he accept Krishna's offer of the Sudarshan Chakra?

Here I am limiting my questions to the Kurukshetra battle only, and those parts which you may have seen even on the TV show, because you surely have not read epic, else I would have also asked —

  1. Why Karna retreated scared from Draupadi’s Swayamvara battle, comparing Arjuna to Rama, Indra and Vishnu.
  2. Why better warrior fled Gandharvas and not so great Arjuna saved the day for Kauravas?
  3. Why better warrior Karna fled Arjuna thrice in Virata war?

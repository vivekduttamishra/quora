If Drona had given Arjuna most of his celestial weapons, then what were the astras that he learned in the Indra Loka?

The most important part of this question is the word “his”.

Drona gave most of his (Drona’s) weapon to Arjuna. But that wasn’t the end of the world. For example Drona didn’t have Gandiva or Pashupata or Indra’s weapon or Kubera’s weapon for that matter. After winning the grace of the Mahadeva Shiva other Gods gave their weapon to Arjuna making him not only invincible but also unique.

On a side note, Weapons often make people powerful, arrogant and trigger happy. In case of Arjuna, it only proved him more worthy, responsible and reasonable. He didn’t use most of them through out his life. There were other warriors of the era who would beg or trade for weapons and whatever their limited arsenal may consist of, they would be so trigger happy that they would use up every single weapon.

Why did Krishna only assist Pandavas, and what is the relationship between them?

📅 Jun 07, 2022 01:42 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

WRONG QUESTION

First things first, while the relationship existed between Krishna and Pandavas, that wasn’t the reason Krishna assisted them. In fact, Krishna tried to help everyone, even the wretched Karna and Duryodhana. And that shows the true nature of God’s help. It comes to everyone. Evils forsake that hand as eagerly as good people accept them.

Now let me expand on my answer.

Krishna’s relationship with Kauravas and Pandavas

Krishna and Pandavas were cousins. Pandava’s mother Kunti was the elder sister of Vasudeva, the father of Krishna. And the same relationship bounded Krishna to even Karna. Incidentally there existed a relationship between Kauravas and Krishna. Krishna’s son was married to Duryodhana’s daughter. Similarly Krishna’s sister was married to Arjuna.

Thus there existed multiple relationship between Krishna and both Kaurvas and Pandavas.

Why Krishna didn’t offer the assistance of Kauravas?

The entire army of Krishna fought for Kauravas. Was that not an assistance? Duryodhana was only too happy to have that army. And before the battle Krishna approached Kaurava with a peace offer which was heavily skewed in favour of Kauravas. Had Kauravas agreed, Krishna assured Pandavas would be friendly with them and the catastrophe could be averted. Alas!

Krishna also tried to assist Karna and offered him Kingship to avert the war. But Karna rejected Krishna’s proposal.

On the other hand when Duryodhana and Arjuna both had approached Krishna for help, he immediately agreed to help them both and Arjuna got the first choice between a non-combatant Krishna and the entire Narayanai Sena. Without a blink he chose Krishna.

Unlike Karna, he didn’t offer him Kingship or any proposition of wealth or luxary. Yet Arjuna selected Krishna.

And their lies the difference. Krishna was a God. He wasn’t partial. But evils are biased against God. They find it difficult to grasp the extended hand and grace of the God. And they suffer for their evil design.

If the truce trials of Srikrishna had succeded and had Duryodhana accepted to cede Indraprastha kingdom to Pandavas and war did not happen, who would be most dissatisfied and for what reasons? If possible can you list them in descending order?

📅 Aug 16, 2022 11:31 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

According to me, the list is a really short one. Karna and Duryodhana.

Karna

He was one of the very few people (in fact the only one) completely vested in war. He cherished the prospect of a battle with Arjuna from the gurukul days and that's what made him demand Brahmastra from Drona, deceive Parshurama and led the Brahmana to curse him. It was his warmongering nature, and not participation, that was rebuked by Krioacharya during talent showcase ceremony.

While he too was wicked, he strived for any opportunity to a battle as long as it deemed safe.

It was his advice that led to ghosha battle and he was the one to second Shusharma for Gharana conspiracy.

Finally Karna rejected the request by both Kunti and Krishna to avoid bloodshed and whatever he may say, it wasn't for Duryodhana but for the war.

Had Duryodhana agreed to peace, Karna would surely be dissatisfied the most, albeit for a little while only.

Duryodhana

Duryodhana was right when he said, he won't give something as significant as a spec of dust to Pandavas. But he wasn't a war monger. He was a deceitful evil and a reluctant fighter. He would have abstained from battle to fight another day. He too would have been dissatisfied for a short while .

Why Dhritrashtra, Duhsashana and shakuni won't be dissatisfied?

These three while evil, lacked original ambition. Their evil acts were for Duryodhana. If he was satisfied, they would be satisfied for that short while.

.

WHY PANDAVAS WON'T BE DISSATISFIED?

Pandavs followed Dharma and didn't trade it for their greed, personal vengeance or anything. If they wanted Kauravas destroyed by any means, they would have achieved it long ago. They could have let them get captured and languish at the hand of Gandharvas or Arjuna could have killed them instead of making them just unconscious in Matasya battle. But they followed Dharma and honoured agreement. They would have done that again and without a blink during that short while.

What is “that short while” I am referring?

Well this is the time between the peace treaty and the next misadventure of the evil duo that would have made the war emminent.

You see, ONLY TWO PEOPLE HAD ORIGINAL STAKE IN BATTLE — DURYODHANA'S GREED AND KARNA'S LUST FOR UNDUE RECOGNITION.

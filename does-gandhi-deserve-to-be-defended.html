Does Gandhi deserve to be defended?

📅 Feb 19, 2020 10:34 PM PST  |  🌐 English

Does Gandhi deserve to be defended? From whom?

No. He does not need a defence — we do. We need to be defend our society from a new era of senseless history being written on WhatsApp, Facebook and propagated to those who are too shy or too afraid to look in the history, whom you can make believe that Gandhi’s glorification is a political propaganda for a political party.

No. He doesn’t need defence from someone who questions if he deserved to be defended. He already has a huge team of defenders including — Dalali Lama to Barak Obama, Albert Einstien to Steeve Jobs, Martin Luthar King Jr to Nelson Mandela, From Dr Rajendra Prasad to Narendra Modi.

No. He doesn’t need a defence — we do, if we don’t realize his worth. From time imemorial India has been belittled — our epics are mocked as mythology, Kalidas was labelled as Indian Shakespaer. For the first time in history, people feel pride to compare their leaders to Gandhi as — Russian Gandhi, African Gandhi and so on. For the first time a world leader compares Gandhi to Buddha and Jeasus, for the first time great non-political minds like Einstien, George Bernard Shaw and Steeve Jobs gets facinated by a black naked fakir.

No. He doesn’t need a defence — Our constitution provides a defence to the likes of Kasab, Godsey and the rapist of Nirbhaya without asking if they need a defence and we have a question here if our father of the nation need a defence.

No. He doesn’t need a defence. We need a defence.

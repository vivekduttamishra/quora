Why did Savarkar break the monopoly of Nehru-Gandhi history books?

📅 Jul 22, 2022 08:16 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Did Savarkar break some monopoly?

History is not some kind of trade or business where you break monopoly. It is not a social media either where you can buy followers, neither is it a time travel pulp fiction where you go and change the past.

It is the reality. Not some fiction book. The world was watching then with rapt attention. World remembers even today. Can you change the world perception and make them believe in some fictional story? Can you make Albert Einstein recant his words or transfer them as tribute to someone else? Can you make Shubhash Chandra Bose call someone else the father of the nation? Can you replace Gandhi's statues, stamps, celebration and name from the world's perception? If not, how would you erase him from the history? And as long as he lives, I doubt you can transfer his legacy to someone else.

Can you credit Mr Savarkar for the nation building, when he had zero role in it? Can you erase the strong condemnation of Savarkar and Jinnah by Bose for supporting the Britishers? Can you ignore the leadership and vision that gave us IIT, IIM, AIIMS, infrastructure and nuclear projects? If not how do you challenge history?

You may rewrite the books and may run social campaign like this one. You can't change the world history. Its beyond anyone's control.

WRONG And derogatory question.

Draupadi wasn't serving. She was wife of five Pandavas who respected her and her boundaries. She wasn't a slave or a servant.

I believe that the question roots from contemporary narrative that suggests Draupadi had her time divided for a year with each husband. No such time period is mentioned in the ancient narrative.

A simple rule existed that anytime Draupadi was with one of her husbands others won't breach their privacy. This is a great example of mutual respect and Arjuna went to exile for breaking this rule.

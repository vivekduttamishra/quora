How did Arjun defeat Karna, Drona, and Bhishma so easily in the Virat War as they were better than him?

📅 Apr 26, 2020 07:31 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A

This question is a leading one with certain Pre assumptions without any basis, and is completely false.

So let me compare Arjuna with others in all wars other than the Virat.

Arjuna

  • How many others had pleased Lord Shiva to gain his grace and the mighty Pashupatastra? He earned the blessings and weapons not stole it.
  • He won against the armies of Drupad imprisoning him? One of the three other warrior named here tried and failed.
  • After Draupadi Swaymber he was attacked by one of them but the challenger retreated after comparing Arjuna with Ram, Indra and Personification of art of war.
  • When Gandharvas attacked Kuru forces and its mightiest fighter fled the war, it was Arjuna who finally defeated Gandharvas forces.
  • The Entire Kuru forces including two of the warriors named here
    • Couldn't even win against Abhimanyu whom they killed in a pack and Ghatotkatch who was slain by Indrastra.
    • Failed to protect Jaidradh from the wrath of Arjun.
  • Arjun killed Jaidradh with entire Kuru forces guarding him.
  • Had been invincible in all his battles.

Drona

  • Which war had Drona won to be considered a warrior better than Arjun?
  • Why he waited for Arjuna to be out of reach before forming Chakravyuh?
  • Why he failed against Abhimanyu, Ghatotkatch and Bheem?

Bhishma

  • himself said before the Virata war that Arjun is invincible. Kripacharya endorsed him.

Note: My answer is based on the story of Mahabharat an Indian epic and refers to English translation by Kisari Mohan Ganguli (KMG), Bibek Debroy and Sanskrit Hindi version of Gita Press.

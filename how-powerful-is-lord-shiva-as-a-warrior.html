How powerful is Lord Shiva as a warrior?

📅 Mar 15, 2020 12:55 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Shiva is not a warrior — he is the UNIVERSE and beyond. He is the cause and not the action. The creation comes in to existence and then returns to nothingness by his wishes. He is Anadi, Anant and Devaadhidev — the Supreme being. Whom would he fight? Everything belongs to him.

He is the source of power in the universe. He is no warrior because there is no one to stand against him — he is one without any comparison —

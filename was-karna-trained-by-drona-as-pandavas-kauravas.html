Was Karna trained by Drona as Pandavas & Kauravas?

📅 Oct 27, 2020 12:14 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

Short answer yes. This is clearly mentioned not only in Adi Parva where Drona is introduced as the guru, but also cross referenced in greater details in Vana Parva and again in Shanti Parva. Vana Parva also mentions Kripacharya as his guru. Drona taught him along side Kaurava's, Amdhakas and Vrishnis.

The only thing he was denied was the knowledge of Brahmastra, which incidentally was denied to other students also. Drona granted this knowledge exclusively to Arjuna when he saved his life while others looked on.

So this can't be classified as biased against Karna, as others too didn't get the knowledge. He was denied because he wanted to misuse the weapon against his rival. Higher knowledge requires not only skill but psychological and character strength also.

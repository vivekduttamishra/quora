If Lord Krishna says he is within us as our inner voice, then why isn’t he able to answer my questions?

📅 Jan 23, 2021 12:56 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Are you sure he isn’t able to answer your question? Is it possible that he is answering and you are not listening? For inner voice to pass through, the passage must be clear — a clear conscience, and no doubt.

But your question suggests that you doubt his ability rather than your inability to understand his subtle messages. Try to be earnest in your question and seek the answer. The answer, if not altered by your selfish temptation would be his.

What was the reason Karna did not use his divine weapon during the war, especially against Arjuna?

📅 Aug 15, 2022 09:30 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

WRONG QUESTION. Understandable as it is auto generated by Quora bot and it doesn’t understand Mahabharata.

Karna used every single of his divine weapons during the battle at different point in time. He made his intention clear that he would use his weapons during Matasya battle, and there is no contradiction that he didn’t use them before running away from Arjuna. Before his death he used not only Brahmastra and Varunastra against Arjuna but also an evil snake against him as naagastra, Vasvi shakti and most of his divine weapons against Ghatotkatch and Bhargavastra against ordinary soldiers.

In fact the first criteria to fight with a divine weapon is to stay put and fight and that was one of the most difficult thing for Karna to accomplish— not to run away! He ran from the battle so often and so frequently that he rarely got time to use his divine weapons.

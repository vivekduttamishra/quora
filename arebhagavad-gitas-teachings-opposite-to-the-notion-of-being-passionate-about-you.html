AreBhagavad Gita's teachings opposite to the notion of being passionate about your work?

📅 Mar 09, 2020 12:24 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

On the contrary the teaching of Bhagwat Gita emphasize on work so much that he compares duty to Dharma. What Krishna suggest is not to be passionate about the outcome that would defocus you from the work. He clearly refers that while he doesnt really need to do anything he still discharges his duty. He also mentions it is impossible not to work so do it in a focused way without worrying about the consequences.

Why did Nathuram Godse assassinate Gandhi?

📅 Feb 22, 2020 11:49 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Gandhiji's murder reasons are the tragedy which can be compared with Julius Caesar and present day anti CAA movement.

Julius Caesar was killed by a group of honorable men (so they claimed) in the national interest. They said he is going to throw away democracy and become a monarch. It didn't matter to them that

he hath brought many captives home to rome,whose ransoms did the general coffers fill.did this in ceasar seem ambitious?

I thrice presented him a kingly crown, Which he did thrice refuse. Was this ambition?

Yet Brutus says he was ambitious, And sure he is an honourable man

— Ref: Julius Caesar by William Shakespeare (speech by Mark Antony)

The tragedy was - he was killed for what he may have done and not what he did.

Today anti CAA protesters have no argument against CAA, yet they are protesting against what may happen tomorrow when NCR comes — tragedy.

Now coming to Gandhiji — he never fasted to give Pakistan any money. The 9 point charter of his last fast is a historical document. Yet our honorable friends say…

He did had a vague plan to go to Pakistan — to put and end to senseless violence as he had a great success in Kolkata. Mind you he couldn't have pressurised Indian government. He wanted peace across border and religion. We must remember, not so long ago cross the border was India and people Indians. Yet our honorable friends say…

You all saw our home minister Amit Shah, while presenting CAB, quoting Gandhiji's concerned for trapped hindus and Shikhs in Pakistan. He was worried for them and hoped his visit to Pakistan would help them. And yet our honorable friends say…

Godse robbed India of its peace and and scope of our Barlin wall falling. Yet he was tried just for a single murder — Tragedy!

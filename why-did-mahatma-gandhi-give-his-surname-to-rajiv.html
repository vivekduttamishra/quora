Why did Mahatma Gandhi give his surname to Rajiv?

📅 Apr 28, 2021 12:33 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

The answer may be a big surprise to you.

Mahatma Gandhi held no patent on Gandhi surname and was not his exclusive property. There were Gandhi before and after him. Rajiv was one such. The two were not bloodline and Mahatma Gandhi had no say here.

Do you think Aham Sharma portrayed the character of Karna accurately in The Star Plus 2013 Mahabharata Epic?

📅 May 25, 2020 07:41 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

It is not possible for an actor to accurately portray a character unless the script and director allows it. So I can't blame Aham Sharma for failing to portray the character accurately. It is show that should be blamed.

Neither Karna's physical appearance not character matched Karna from Vyas Mahabharat. In Mahabharat Karna rebukes Bheem as clean shaven one (void of mustache). Now Aham Sharma (and all other characters have taken Bheema as style icon and clean shaven.

One of the most challenging role to play would be a gray character — strong, proud, jealous, arrogant, defeated. Few character had so much scope. But when you change dark gray to bright white, in terms of photography it's over exposure and in overexposure the detailing and beauty is often and completely lost. Same has happened with this character here.

I think given a chance, he might have delivered a good performance.

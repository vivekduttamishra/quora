Who are Karna fans on Quora and who are Arjun fans?

📅 Mar 06, 2021 12:42 AM PST  |  🌐 English

I am not sure, who are the fans of Karna and Arjuna on Quora. But I can tell you who are fans/appreciators of Karna and Arjuna in the epic. Here is a list.

Fans of Karna

  • Karna… He is one of his greatest fan, with extreme and unrealistic confidence in his own calibre, which almost every time proved and grossly overestimation.
  • Duryodhana… He is again a great fan of Karna with unwavering trust on Karna, at least till 14th day of Mahabharata.
  • Kauravas and Dhritrasthra… Although his trust was mostly based on the trust of Duryodhana.

Fans of Arjuna

  • Krishna… While Krishna spoke about greatness of other warriors on a few occasions, he marked Arjuna as his inseparable existence and described him in superlative terms.
  • Parashurama… The great warrior called Arjuna the greatest warrior with no equivalent. He two deemed Krishna-Arjuna invanquishible.
  • Mahadeva Shiva… Shiva, in no unclear terms, mentioned Arjuna as the greatest warrior and endorsed his victory.
  • Dronacharya … The great guru compared Arjuna to Indra and like the younger brother of Indra in archary.
  • Ashwatthama… Deemed Arjuna as great warrior.
  • Karna … Probably the greatest fan of Arjuna was none other than Karna himself. He idolized Arjuna all his life and wanted to be better than him. His entire life revolved around Arjuna.
  • Ved Vyas … Called Arjuna as an incarnation of sage nara and called him the trunk of the tree of righteousness.
  • All his friends and enemies… All his enemies from Duryodhana to Dhritrashtra to Bhagdatta had a grudging admiration and fear from Arjuna.

I believe the right question isn’t who are the fans of Karna and Arjuna. The right question is who should be followed — Karna or Arjuna? What is your view?

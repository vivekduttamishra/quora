If there was a war between Adolf Hitler with his Nazis and Pandavas in India, Who will win the war and who will be demolished and Butchered away? Can Pandavas be victorious by demolishing Nazis and butchering Hitler?

📅 Mar 01, 2022 06:25 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

Let me answer you question in too parts.

WHO WOULD WIN THE WAR BETWEEN PANDAVAS AND NAZIS?

Pandavas, without an iota of doubts. Arjuna alone fought forces that were much larger and stronger than Nazis. The strength of Nazis army was 13.6 million during world war II. Just four Pandavas fought billions of Gandharavas who had more sophisticated and devastating weapons than those present during WW II. Even today, we don’t have a single weapon that can destroy entire Universe. Then Arjuna alone defeated several millions of Nivatakavacha army and these are some of his few victories.

But most important, Nazis were brought down by mere mortals using ordinary weapons and Pandavas were unvanquished. You may consider a Nazi army fighting superman.

WHO WOULD BE BUTCHERED AWAY?

No one unless Nazis tried to show their foolhardy. Pandavas fought ethical battles and allowed defeated enemies to retreat if they surrendered or were unable to fight. There are several examples —

  • Panavas defeated Drupada during Gurudakshina, but doesn’t butcher their army. In fact they handled the situation so gracefully that Drupad secretly wished Arjuna to be his son in law.
  • Arjuna defeated another Gandharva but once he got defeated, Arjuna spared his life gracefully.
  • Arjuna defeated Karna during Draupadi’s swaymvar and yet when the later wanted to withdraw, He doesn’t pursue him to kill. He let the scared Karna flee.
  • Arjuna defeated Gandharvas but he found that Chitrasena was fighting feebly he gracefully ended the battle.
  • Arjun defeated Bhagdatta during Rajsuy yagna battle, but when he approached after getting defeated Arjuna once again was graceful not to attack him.
  • Arjuna defeated and rendered entire Kuru army unconscious after the Matasya battle. But then he doesn’t butcher them.
  • Arjuna spared the life of Ashwatthama, who treacherously murdered his entire next generation and the families of Draupadi.

All these are sufficient incidents to prove Pandavas weren’t war mongers hell bent in destroying or butchering enemies.

But…

Nazis may have still been butchered

Weather Nazis would be butchered or not depended on Nazis. Pandavas waged ethical war as long as possible. But there were instances where opponent refused to relent and that resulted in their total destruction. Example would be the destruction of Nivatkavacha and Khandavadahana.

TAKEAWAY

Pandva would have won without a doubt and even in victory they would be gracious enough not to butcher the army. If the army ever got butchered, the onus would be on them and NOT on Pandavas.

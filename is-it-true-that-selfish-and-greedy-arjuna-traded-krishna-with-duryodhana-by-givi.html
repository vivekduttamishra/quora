Is it true that Selfish and Greedy Arjuna traded Krishna with Duryodhana by giving him Narayani Sena in exchange of Krishna so that he could win the war and get throne? How did Arjuna became businessmen who did magnificent trade of God?

📅 Feb 22, 2021 10:37 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Oh Yes, You made your point. You hate Arjuna and wanted to point that out in no subtle tone. What’s next? Do you really need an answer to this question?

Answer is simple — You are no Krishna. And had you cared to learn about Krishna, you would understand, no one can trade Krishna — wise or fool, cunning or smart. The only way to win Krishna was to surrender yourself, a price Duryodhana couldn’t have paid, and had he paid, there would have been no war.

If you read the epic, Krishna offered his Sudarshan Chakra and Ashwatthama, only if he could lift it. And the fool couldn’t lift them. Evils, Greedy and Selfish can’t get the lord. Here is what Krishna said —

advesta sarva-bhutanam
maitrah karuna eva ca
nirmamo nirahankarah
sama-duhkha-sukhah ksami
santustah satatam yogi
yatatma drdha-niscayah
mayy arpita-mano-buddhir
yo mad-bhaktah sa me priyah

—Gita Chapter 12, Shloka 13,14

One who is not envious but who is a kind friend to all living entities, who does not think himself a proprietor, who is free from false ego and equal both in happiness and distress, who is always satisfied and engaged in devotional service with determination and whose mind and intelligence are in agreement with Me-he is very dear to Me.

And Krishna, in Mahabharata says — Arjuna is an inseparable existence to him. He who loves Arjuna, loves me. He who hates Arjuna hates me.

What does that tell you? Arjuna was greedy and selfish? What about Krishna? A moron, who can be traded between Arjuna and Duryodhana?

Wakeup. And see what Krishna thinks of those who hate Arjuna! Do you think, he would have sided with Duryodhana? Do you think, Duryodhana could have grasped his purity?

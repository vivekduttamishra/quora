Short Answer

  1. Did Draupadi refused to marry Karna — No. only 5 out of 1259 ancient scripts refers to this incident and most important of them Neelkantha includes its contradictions as well.
  2. Did Karna failed to string the bow — YES. Mentioned in all the important narrations including BORI, KMG, Southern and other recitations. There are few overstretched arguments that Karna that failed wasn’t this Karna. But those arguments are limited to few Quora posts only and have no base in either epic or authentic versions.
  3. Did Karna failed to hit target — YES — But because he failed to string the bow.

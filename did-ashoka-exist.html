Did Ashoka exist?

📅 Mar 29, 2021 01:33 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

Oh, yes! The great King Ashoka existed.

People may not even bother to ask us if we existed, few years or few decades after we are gone. But Ashoka existed. And as goes the popular saying, it’s carved in stones. And in case of Ashoka, it’s literally carved in stones. Undeniable, unquestionable and completely verifiable.

You can’t use these words for Alexander, the great. Did he exist? Is there sufficient evidence? But you don’t question that.

I don’t know what prompted this question, but the king whose inscriptions inspired our national flag and national symbol existed and walked from being a great warrior of weapons to a great emperor whose message of peace propagated far and wide and almost change the history of entire region and not just one country.

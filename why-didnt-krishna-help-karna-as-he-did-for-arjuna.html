Why didn't Krishna help Karna as he did for Arjuna?

📅 May 07, 2020 09:44 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

This is a great question, and I am answering this more for myself than anyone else.

What is God’s help?

If we read Indian Epics, we find the definition of sins, crimes and punishment a lot different from those imbibed elsewhere.

  1. There is no devil who controls the destiny of the bad guys — One’s own Karma guided by his Gunas (Sato, Rajo, Tamas) controls it. And one can control his Gunas and Karma.
  2. God doesn’t spoonfeed you. They often guide you in subtle ways. They are not your assistants; they come disguised and give you a lesson.
  3. God doesn’t choose you, you choose to be with the God.

Did Krishna helped Arjuna at every step?

  1. Krishna doesn’t come to rescue Arjuna from Lakshagrih.
  2. Krishna doesn’t fight for Arjuna in Draupadi Swyamber, when a bunch of angry men, having failed to win Draupadi attacked him.
  3. Krishna doesn’t protect him in the game of dice.
  4. Krishna doesn’t prevent the circumstances of his exile.
  5. Krishna doesn’t help him in the Virat war.

Didn’t Krishna helped Karna and Duryodhana?

A careful reading of Mahabharat, would reveal that Krishna not only helped Karna, but also Duryodhana. But as I said earlier, Not that Krishna didn’t try to help them, they refused to seek his help.

  1. Didn’t Krishna came to Hastinapur with a peace treaty which was heavily biased in favor of Duryodhana? Wasn’t that a help?
  2. Krishna showed his divine form to Duryodhana even before he blessed Arjuna. How did Duryodhana react? How did Karna react?
  3. Didn’t Krishna offer Karna a chance to become the savior of humanity by preventing the war? Didn’t he ask him to switch side to Dharma — to come to his fold? Karna could have heeded his advice and crossed sides to Dharma and Krishna. But Alas…
  4. Didn’t Krishna offered himself unarmed vs his army. Agreed, Duryodhana didn’t get to choose first, but wasn’t he too happy and Arjuna’s naïve selection?
  5. Didn’t Krishna tried to help Karna realize err of his ways just before his last moment? Had he surrendered even then…

God extends help to everyone, only blessed follow his lead.

From another perspective, do you seriously think Lord Krishna should have switch sides and sided with Adharma to help Karna? Why?

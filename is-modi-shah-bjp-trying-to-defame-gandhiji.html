Is Modi-Shah & BJP trying to defame Gandhiji?

📅 Oct 04, 2021 06:35 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

I don’t think this question has a straight or a simple answer.

Let us understand no one in top leadership can be that stupid to defame Mahatma Gandhi. No Indian Prime Minister can afford to do that and not make a ridicule of himself across international community, who regards highly of Mahatma Gandhi.

Coming to Narendra Modi, I can see his genuine admiration for Mahatma Gandhi, which is not just a political gimmick. And not only has he admired Gandhiji in words, but also by his action. While his ambitious plan of Swatch Bharata Abhiyana was a direct tribute to the father of the nation, his other agenda of empowering the people too have an impression of Gandhian thought.

Today, I don’t see many leaders in India, who are as close to Gandhian philosophy as Narendra Modi is.

And not only from top BJP brass but also RSS Chief Mohan Bhagwat have spoken about Mahatma Gandhi which shows his high regard for the man who was a staunch Hindu by his birth and action.

That said, BJP doesn’t comprises of only stalwarts like Narendra Modi but lacks of mediocre leaders and workers with little knowledge of truth and history. In their limited intellect Mahatma Gandhi was some distant ancestors contemporary first family of congress. For them insulting Mahatma Gandhi is akin to insulting contemporary Gandhi. Little do they know, nothing could be more distant from the truth. They do not know Gandhiji and Gandhiji won’t have recognized them. But as I said people with such narrow mindset won’t understand this simple difference.

A malicious propaganda continues to defame Mahatma Gandhi with absurd and made up stories. They would praise anyone who was even slightly averse to Gandhi and often try to show conflict where non existed.

We have seen public outburst of Narendra Modi against his own party leader when the later tried to insult Gandhiji. He said, he would never forgive her. But unfortunately this conspiracy has taken it’s root so strong that I daresay even Modiji is helpless to curb it completely. Interestingly few of those BJP workers even speak ill of Modiji for having such an admiration for Mahatma Gandhi.

Guess whoever admires Mahatma Gandhi today is condemned to be at the receiving hands from people who have been unfortunately misled by those propagandas.

What dangerous things can Karna fans do?

📅 Apr 02, 2022 11:36 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

That is an interesting question and we have a lot of interesting answers below. But I would like to present a different perspective.

No matter how many illogic Karna’s fans may present, they can’t change the past. They can not make Krishna drive the chariot for Karna. They can’t make Shiva pronounce Karna as the greatest warrior in the three worlds. But they can seriously damage the future and send false impression about our culture to the world.

Our Culture and Our Values

What Karna fan doesn’t realize is they are not just glorifying Karna or demeaning Arjuna, they are mocking our ancient culture, for if Karna was really a good person then Krishna’s must be bad enough to get him killed. And then someone with zero understanding of relegion and culture would say — Behold, they worship a false God. What does that reflect on us and our moral values?

Gray Characters

They try to imply Mahabharata was a story of gray characters. They don’t realize that gray is large spectrum with one end almost white and another almost black. And then theory contradicts Krishna’s eternal statement — Partiranaya Sadhunama, Vinashchays Duskritam.

Now who is Sadhu and who is Dushta when everyone is gray? They mock the basis premise of the ancient epic that says truth triumphs. I don’t know how any idol worshipping can make a man stoop so low as to destroy the very fabric that sustains them.

ACID ATTACKER CULTURE

They tend to justify Karna’s provocation and role in moestation of Draupadi or his involvement in trying to burn down Draupadi alive after the swaymvar as a retailiation to Draupadi rejecting him.

Today, I would not re-emphasize on the fact that it has been critically accepted beyond doubt that Druaupadi never rejected Karna. You may check my previous answers on that question.

Today let us understand what this justification means. Let us assume that completely fabricated story to be true. Let us assume Draupadi rejected Karna. Now does that give a man rejected by a woman, right to molest her? Does that justify his attempt to burn her down? How is this any different from modern perverts who throw acid on girls they claim to love?

Justifying Karna is not just justifying them but also make them herors. Karna, perhaps is the first recorded acid attacker in our history. What should we expect from his fans?

DECEITFUL STUDENTS

Karna is also a hero for all those deceitful students who think it is their birth right to get everything without hardwork. They have right to resent and cheat because they can’t compete the topper and want equal marks awarded without equal hard effort. They think it is only fair to cheat and fabricate. They can’t enter IIM because the interview board rejected it, so they can fabricate certificates and take an entry and them blame when they are busted by calling IIM is having a descriminatory policy.

They explain it is ok to bite the very hand that feed them and consider deceit as a way to justify action.

To destroy a nation, you don’t always need Divyastra. Just corrupt their education system and ethical values. People would become animals and justify beastly instincts.

POWER HUNGER

Most people who idolize Karna claim that he was very powerful and a great archer. They count some of his victories. But they couldn’t explain how those so called great power helped humanity. They talk about power but not it’s implication. There is no mention that Karna ever did any good to humanity. He never saved a woman in distresss who evils were trying to burn. He never saved a village from a demon. He never tried to protect a woman from molestation. He was never helped a Kingdom from looters attacking them. Hell, he didn’t even help his own friend and brothers from enemy attack. Naturally go never chose him for any of their missions.

Even if we have to assume Karna had great strength, question is what worth is that strength for society?

Should we idolize a person, just because he was powerful? Or is being good also important? Should we justify Putin’s action because he is powerful? Should be clap for USA’s attack on Japan because they were powerful?

There was a time when India taught the world, the value of being Good.

The greatest danger is, can we save our culture.

AN APPEAL

I would like to appeal to all Karna fans. Take a pause. Think. Ask yourself, what Karna ever did for humanity. Ask yourself, do you really think he deserves your hero worshipping? What does this tell about your morale compass? Do not devalue yourself, your culture and your country by bestowing your trust on such an evil man as Karna.

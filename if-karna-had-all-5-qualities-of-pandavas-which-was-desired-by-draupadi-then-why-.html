If Karna had all 5 qualities of Pandavas, which was desired by Draupadi, then why didn't Krishna reveal the real identity of Karna in Swayamvara and let him marry Draupadi?

📅 Mar 13, 2020 10:16 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

This is one of those wonderful question which contains its own answer. And the key part of this question is the very first word — if

Now Vyasji forgot to mention this fact that Karna had all qualities of 5 Pandavas taken together. Let us analyse if he had the qualities of all 5 Pandavas —

  1. Yudhisthir — He followed Dharma and never lied except once in his life time. Karna was a liar. He lied to his own guru and was cursed.
  2. Bhim — Was the only person to have the strength of a 1000 elephant. Since he was the only one, naturally Karna couldn’t complete. He proved his might by killing Hidimbasur, Bakasur, Kichak, Jarasandh and all Kauravas? Where was Karna in the competition? He was a dutiful son. Was Karna dutiful? Is there a clue in Bakasur story?
  3. Arjun — Was blessed by his guru as a devoted pupil, Karna was cursed being deceitful. Arjun earned the blessings of Shiva. Arjun defeated Drupad where Karna failed. Arju didn’t resent driving chariot during Virata war. Can Karna beat them? Arjun was rated Atirathi, Karna a Adhirathi.
  4. Nakul, Sahdev — Were dutiful brothers and sons without personal ambitions or egos. Karna was full of personal egos and ambition to prove himself better than Arjun.

Look carefully and you Karna wasn’t comparable to one of the Pandavas in quality unless you calculate from the reverse side and compare Yudhisthir’s archery and Nakul’s strength.

Lord Krishna was wise beyond mortal understanding and he chose not to side with Karna — Do we need more proof?

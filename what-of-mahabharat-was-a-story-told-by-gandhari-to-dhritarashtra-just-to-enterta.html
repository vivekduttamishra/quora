What of Mahabharat was a story told by Gandhari to Dhritarashtra just to entertain him?

📅 Apr 21, 2020 09:13 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

This question seem to be inspired by 1001 Arabian Nights and it has more than 1001 holes.

Gandhari tells Dhritrashtra following tale —

  • He was born blind in a most unconvincing manner, and naturally Dhirtrashtra being blind doesn’t know anything about his birth or blindness.
  • He has hundred sons about which of course he has no idea.
  • He must have been highly entertained to know that his sons were all evil.
  • That he partitioned his kingdom for the fun of listening a story!
  • That his sons were deceitful and tried to disrobe a women in front of him and he did nothing.
  • That all his sons finally died dishonored

Really entertaining story!

I am not sure what is more stupid, that this question was asked or that I actually answered it.

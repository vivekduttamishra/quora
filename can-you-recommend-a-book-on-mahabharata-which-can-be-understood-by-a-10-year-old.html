Can you recommend a book on Mahabharata which can be understood by a 10-year-old kid?

📅 Nov 10, 2020 10:55 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A. I would recommend two for a 10-year-old-kid. —

  1. Mahabharata series from Amar Chitra Katha. You won't find it distorted like modern narratives in TV shows, though few small deviations are likely.
  2. Mahabharata by Rajgopalachari. It's an abridged version with simple English with one core goal — present Mahabharata as it is.

If these two sources manages to attract your kid to greatest tale ever told, he would find ways to get more sources.

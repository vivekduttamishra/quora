Why did Nara-Narayana didn't kill Kartavirya Arjuna?

📅 Jan 25, 2021 07:20 AM PST  |  🌐 English

When I read the question, it took me a second to understand the motive. But then I must question back —

  • Why should only Nara and Narayana vanquish all the evils?
  • Should they always work together?
  • If a particular evil is not destroyed by Nara and Narayana, does that mean Nara and Narayana doesn’t exist or they don’t matter?

In fact anyone who dares to fight the evil is an incarnate of Narayana, for Narayana is the protector Vishnu.

By the way, Kartavirya Arjuna was the destroyed by Bhagwan Parshurama, the sixth incarnation of Narayana Vishnu.

Hope that helps.

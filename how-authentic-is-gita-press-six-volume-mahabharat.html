Gita Press Mahabharata is primarily based on Neelkantha edition which is also the primary source for KMG. What makes it more relevant is the fact that it cross references BORI Mahabharata at places where it is required. This makes it more authentic and critical than KMG which was translated Pre BORI era. Being focused on single primary source makes it closer to ancient version.

Another important advantage of Gita Press is its Sanskrit Hindi translation is done by an eminent Sanskrit scholar Pandit Ram Narayan Shastri while the most common source of BORI is the English translation by Bibek Debroy who is an economist and have some known translation issues.

This makes Gita Press version one of the most accurate and reliable version.

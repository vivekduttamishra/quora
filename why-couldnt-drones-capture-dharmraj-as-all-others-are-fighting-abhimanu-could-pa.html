Why couldn’t drones capture Dharmraj as all others are fighting Abhimanu? Could Pandavas be able to resist all day?

📅 Jan 21, 2022 06:54 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Dharmo Rakshati Rakshitaah!

Dharma protects those who protect Dharma.

Let us take a step back. Drona knew he couldn’t win the battle as long as Arjuna was around. He was right. He thought if only he could if only Arjuna can be taken out of equation, he would win. He was wrong.

To answer this question comprehensively let us evaluate the battles a day before and a day after before visiting the exact day.

Drona put his plan to action on Day 12. To keep Arjuna away a special task force was deployed. Fourteen thousand Samshaptaka laid down their life so that Drona can fulfil his mission. What did Drona get it return? Nada!

Not let us fast forward to Day 14. The objective was to protect Jaidradh till the day end hoping that Arjuna would kill himself, because killing him was beyond anyone’s capacity in the Kuru camp. And Drona with his entire Army couldn’t stop Arjuna. Once again. Before you think, this to be just Arjuna effect, you need to think again.

On the day 14, Arjuna didn’t fight alone. Bhimasena continuously defeated Drona and Karna. Drona couldn’t even stop Satyaki. And the war ended in a total disaster for Kauravas. They lost all their hopes of victory on this very day. Remaining four days was just formal closure.

Now having set the context let us turn to day 13. One of the key piece of Drona’s chakravyuh was Jaidradh and his mystic power owing to Shiva’s boon. Drona felt confident that Pandava’s sans Arjuna won’t stand a chance in his chakravyuh. Arjuna alone was immune to both the chakravyuha and Jaidradha’s mystic power, again by Shiva’s exception in his boon.

Thus Drona formed his Chakravyuha. He thought Pandava’s helpless and weak. In his arrogance he ignored Abhimanyu. He forgot he was trained by Arjuna and Krishna and was immune to Shiva’s boon.

Abhimanyu destroyed the Chakravyuh formation. Now there was no single way to enter and Jaidradh couldn’t be every where. If at this point Drona attempted to capture Yudhishthira, he would have to face Bhimasena and Satyaki. Would he be able to do that? Well that is why I reminded you of day 14. He couldn’t stop these two warriors. Had Drona the capacity to pull a victory he would have done on day 14 when Arjuna was busy destroying a large army far away.

The only possible way Drona could have worked his way was when all three conditions met —

  1. No Arjuna
  2. Chakra Vyuah in motion
  3. Jaidradha stood at guard.

Abhimanyu had no only disrupted the plan he had cause heavy fatality to Kuru army with many prominent people wiped out including Karna’s brother, Duryodhaha’s son, Shalya’s son and son on. He was unstoppable even with Drona around. Without Drona, he was a total disaster. Drona stayed put because —

  1. He couldn’t have managed to capture Yudhishira alone while he was guarded by warriors like Bhimasena and Satyaki.
  2. If he moved he risked losing more of his men at the hands of Abhimanyu. The only thing he could have done is what he did. Stay put and kill Abhimanyu.

What would have been the fate of Mahabharata if Arjun had asked for Lord Krishna's army and arsenal instead of Lord Krishna himself? Would the fate have reversed?

📅 May 14, 2020 11:59 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

First I don't believe in any scenario Arjuna would have been either as stupid or as arrogant to have chosen otherwise. But let us assume for the sake of argument, assume this situation —

The course of the time was guided by Krishna, and while Arjuna played a pivotal role, he wasn't the controller of destiny. No single decision had the capacity to change Krishna's will that was aligned with Dharma.

  1. Pandava's would have gained a large army, Kaurava's would have lost one.
  2. It was beyond Duryodhana to control Krishna. Krishna won't have taken up the arms.
  3. He may have convinced Bhishma to retire sooner.
  4. He may not have allowed execution of Abhimanyu.
  5. Krishna, a ranchod, would never had allowed his ego rule his judgements.
  6. Kauravas wouldn't have got any tactical advantage from Krishna's presense. Those without pure heart can never get Krishna.
  7. If Kaurava general crumbled because of Shalya, think what havoc could Krishna have wrecked.

Moral of the story — Dharma would have won, no matter what.

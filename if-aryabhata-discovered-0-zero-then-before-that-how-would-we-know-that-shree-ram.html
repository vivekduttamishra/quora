There are several ways to understand this simple idea

Approach 1 to understand

imaṁ vivasvate yogaṁ proktavān aham avyayam
vivasvān manave prāha manur ikṣhvākave ’bravīt

evaṁ paramparā-prāptam imaṁ rājarṣhayo viduḥ
sa kāleneha mahatā yogo naṣhṭaḥ parantapa

Gita chapter 4 — sloka 1,2

The first two sloka of 4th chapter of Gita is vital to understand the understanding of the notion of Shruti (श्रुति) and smriti (स्मृति).

Ancient India transmitted knowledge using a continuous human chain, and in the process certain knowledge was lost, to be rediscovered.

Years later Aryabhatt rediscovered 0.

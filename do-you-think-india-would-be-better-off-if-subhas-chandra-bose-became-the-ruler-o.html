Do you think India would be better off if Subhas Chandra Bose became the ruler of independent India?

📅 Jun 12, 2020 09:07 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Shubhash Chandra Bose is a national hero, a great nationalist, strategist and a patriot. He was so extremely determined that he could go to any length to see his vision come true and this is apparent from the fact that he even negotiated with Hitler to form the INA.

But, he was also a communist and a dictator, and he had expressededly advocated a dictatorial regime for India. Now history bears the testimony that these two traits have been disastrous for any nation and specifically a young developing nation. Look no further than Pakistan — they just suffered dictatorial regime not communist and where are they today? Look what communism did to world power like Russia. Imagine where dictatorship + communism would have done to India… A recent proposal by such intellectual think tank proposed that in the name of Pendamic, the government should declare individual citizens’ properties as public property. In such a case, if applied you loose right to your own hard earned property and may have to share your own house with a stranger. This has happened in communist country like Russia. The idea became a non starter only because because we don't have a communist government and have a popular democratic leader.

So, with great regard for Shubhash Chandra Bose, I do believe, India should never get a dictator or communism regime. It must be noted that I am against the school of thought and not against my hero Netaji Shubhash Chandra Bose.

I do wish, however, if he could be the incharge of India's defence…

Jai Hind

Did Nehru choose Shastri as his successor?

📅 Aug 01, 2022 01:25 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

No. Nehru officially didn't choose any successor. Interestingly just three days before his death on answering to this exact question he said, I am not nearing my death yet.

That said, he wasn't in perfect health for quite sometime and had delegated several of his key responsibilities to Lal Bahadur Shastri. While this doesn't mean he declared Shastri ji as his successor, it certainly is a proof of the trust Nehruji had in him.

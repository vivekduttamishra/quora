What would happen if Drona, Bhishma, or Karna, only one of them opposed the Draupadi Vastra Haran? What would be its effect to the whole epic?

📅 Jan 12, 2021 09:48 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

First, one could have expected Drona or Bhisma to oppose vastra haran, and not from Karna, who was in fact the person who suggested in the first place. Then there is nothing about Karna to suggest that he would have ever opposed it, as he was one of the prime conspirators in almost all the conspiracies against Pandavas and some more where Duryodhana wasn’t even involved.

Second, it was certainly expected of Drona or Bhishma to oppose Vastra haran, but there is explicit mention of their silence by Vikarna. Why they remained silent beats me. But I think there is a subtle hint here in what Bhishma response to Draupadi — He wanted to held Yudhishthir responsible.

But even if Drona or Bhishma had interrupted, in all probability Duryodhana would have ignored them and still went on with the proposal of Karna in an attempt to disrobe Draupadi and eventually the attempt would have failed. After all people protested the very moment, Yudhishtir staked Draupadi.

Thus in the long run, there would be almost negligible impact on the epic’s final outcome, but certainly it would have boosted the image of the Bhishma and Drona.

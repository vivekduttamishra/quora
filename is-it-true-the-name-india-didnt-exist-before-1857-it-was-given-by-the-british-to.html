Is it true the name "India" didn't exist before 1857, it was given by the British to replace the previously used word "Subcontinent" or Bar e Sagheer?

📅 Jan 22, 2021 06:51 PM PST  |  🌐 English

Just a few simple facts —

  • Britishers formed East India company in 1600
  • Vasco De Gama discovered first sea root to India in 1498
  • Columbus started his voyge to search for India, ended up landing in America.

When they searched for India, not only it existed for a much longer time but was hailed for its charms that people took pains in search of her.

The word India derives from Indus, which in turn derive from Sindhu (Indus Valley Civilization) one of the most ancient civilization.

India has also been known as Bharat and Aryavart for a very long time.

The Pandavas also made mistakes by insulting Karna and Eklavya, why did Krishna help them in war?

📅 Mar 24, 2020 01:52 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Can we seek the answer from reverse please?

Let's rephrase this way —

Krishna helped Pandavas in the war, does that mean they were good guys and didn't insult Karna and Eklavya?

Congratulations! You got your answer.

  1. Pandavas never had a dealing with Eklavya so they didn't insult him.
  2. When did Pandava insult Karna? Oh yes I just recalled.
    1. Bhim insults Karna after Karna gate crashes the passing out ceremony, uninvited and tried to demean and discredit Arjun. Remember it was an occasion to showcase calibre of Drona's student and Karna had left his gurukul long ago. Drona had not asked him to leave.
    2. Arjuna swears to kill Karna. Can I count it an insult? Oh wasn't it soon after Karna had insulted each Pandavas, humiliated Draupadi and tried to strip her in public (oh, I know Dussasan tried, but you remember it was Karna's suggestion)
    3. Arjun chided him on Day 14, of the war, after Karna humiliated Bhim after defeating him once. Incidentally Bhim didn't humiliate him after defeating him 4 times the same day.

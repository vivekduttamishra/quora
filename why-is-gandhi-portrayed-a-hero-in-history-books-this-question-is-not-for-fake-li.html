Why is Gandhi portrayed a hero in History book?

Because Gandhiji was a hero. He was the face of our freedom movement for decades. It was his efforts that created the concept of Pan India movements. There wasn't really any true nationwide movements before Gandhiji's time. Non-cooperation movement was endorsed by not only masses, and the national and regional leaders, but also by youths of India like great Chandrashekhar Azad.

He remained at the front of his movements, taking the beating and going to jail. His efforts and success in maintain peace in Calcutta during partition is unrivalled in the world history.

When he died contemporary news papers compared it with second curxification in the history of humanity. And history books weren’t written till then.

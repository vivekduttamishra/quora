  • Kunti would have failed to prove that the abandoned baby she found was a Kshatriya. He may not still have become soldier or warrior. May be he would have become a cook.
  • There was no reason that any one would have allowed a unwed princess to adopt the child. He may have been sent to an orphanage or given for adoption to a poorer family unlike rich Adhirath.
  • No wsy Kunti could have disclosed him her son later or he could have got a chance to become the king.
  • He may have remained in Kunti kingdom without living a meaningless existence.

Other Alternatives (Best)

Surya would have understood the predicament of Kunti and not forced his will on her to satisfy his ego. Had he not threatened to destroy Kunti’s family the world would have been a better place with one less evil.

The Last Alternative (Second Best)

Better if the family had a closer tie with royalty of HASTINAPUR, that he may get the best education.

Why is Ghandi more promoted than Quaid-e-Azam?

📅 May 26, 2022 10:29 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

The question is flawed. Neither of the two are ‘products’ that require promotion. They earned their places in history as per their role. Neither of them are pre-historic personalities. They played their part of the same stage with the world watching. Media like radio and newspaper made the foul play extremely difficult. Eminent personalities around the world formed their own opinion based on what they saw. People chose their ideals based on whom they connect to better.

One of them died trying to keep the nation united, the other divided a great nation, its land and its people considering the interest of a specific group of people. One couldn’t protect his own citizen from a minority community (Hindus) from massacre, other tried to cross over the political boundary to protect the victims and died in his dare. One of them practiced a public life where his life was an open book, other’s life threatening disease was a guarded secret for even his own people. I think, it is non-brainer to see whom the world admired.

And if it is still difficult to see, just look at the two nations that carry their legacy. Count Hindu’s in Pakistan and Muslims in India. See their prosperity, education and values and you would get the answer of your question.

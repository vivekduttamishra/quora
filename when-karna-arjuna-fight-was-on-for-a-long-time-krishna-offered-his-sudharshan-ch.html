When Karna-Arjuna fight was on for a long time, Krishna offered his Sudharshan chakra to Arjuna, which tge latter refused. What would have been the impact if Arjuna had killed Karna with Sudharshan Chakra?

📅 Apr 15, 2023 12:41 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

This question expects us to imagine a ‘what if' in an alternative universe by an alterego of Arjuna, so the answer I am going to suggest won't be as per current version of the epic, and yet, there can fewer answers that could be closer to our authentic epic .

Karna would have been very much dead, like Shishupal. No surprises here. But was it possible for Arjuna to handle Sudarshana? Afterall Ashwatthama failed to do so. But then Ashwatthama was no Arjuna. Krishna won't have offered Arjuna Sudarshana if he was unworthy. Krishna didn't call Arjuna an inseparable existence from himself without a reason. Shiva won't have handed him Pashupata. So if Arjuna had taken the Sudarshana, the Kurukshetra would have seen a unified form of two Krishna as one! The manifestation of Nara and Narayana!

But then, Arjuna was part of Krishna. Inseparable part. He would have taken Sudarshana just like Krishna held the chariot wheel against Bheeshma, to bow him down and remind that his end is near. Very very near. And then an ordinary Anjalika would have finished Karna off.

Hi thanks for the A2A.

This incident is not considered as an interpolation and is present in most major versions.

There can be so many different ways to explain it —

  • You don't need divine powers to hear things that happened around you. You learn from various sources. That's the foundation of Shruti tradition.
  • Mahabharata story isn't written in a straight time line rather more as flash backs. So the time of narration need not be in exact order.

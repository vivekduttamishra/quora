Can Karna, Bhishma, Drona, and Ashwathama have the ability to defeat any Deva in a single one-on-one battle?

📅 May 01, 2021 12:31 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

People would say what they want to. Point is, you can say with certainty only what happened.

Bhishma

Among the named warriors, only Bhishma had the distinction of defeating a divinity — Parshurama.

Some may point out that Parshurama had lost his divinity after his encounter with Shri Rama. Yet he never lost the great warrior he was and Mahabharata always refers him in the most superlative terms.

But that is the closes any of these got to defeating any divinity.

Drona

While being a great teacher of weapons, Drona wasn’t a warrior per-se. There is no reference of his involvement in any battle before Matasya battle where he was defeated fair and square by Arjuna along with other Maharathis of Kauravas.

Even when he became general he had a constant and realistic fear of Arjuna and refrained for formation of Chakravyuh till Arjuna was safely out of his sight. We can give him the credit of being accurate in his calculations, but not as a warrior.

Drona also felt overwhelmed on seeing Abhimanyu fight.

So fighting and winning against a Deva is squarely out of question.

Ashwatthama

Ashwatthama was a better fighter than Drona. I won’t call him a warrior as he lacked warrior ethics. But he never did anything to perfection. He wanted to traded his weapon for Krishna’s Sudarshan Chakra and failed to even lift that. To think of that he would have a winning chance against Devas is laughable.

Karna

Unlike Drona and Ashwatthama, things can be said with evidence about Karna. No didn’t have capability to defeat Devas or any warrior who could give him tough time for a little while.

Karna lack perseverance or focus. He had a tendency to flee a battle and he did it more times than any warrior in entire epic. He was mangled and forced to flee by Gandharvas and he pinned his hopes on weapons like Nagastra or Vasishakti to win the battle for him. And when it comes to weapons, Devas certainly had more potent weapons with him.

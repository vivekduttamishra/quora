Why did karna insult draupadi?

📅 Mar 18, 2020 08:37 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Karna insulted Draupadi because —

  • He felt insulted that Arjun won the Swaymber and the grace of Draupadi and he couldn't.
  • I feel the resentment was more against Arjun than Draupadi.
  • There is a popular (but not critically endorsed) that Draupadi rejected him for being Suta. This comes from KMG that takes it from Neelkantha, and later also mentions the alternative story of Karna failing the Swaymber. Story of Karna failing is mentioned in Dakshinayat, Gita Press, Neelkantha footnote, Bori and by Rajgopalachari.
  • But assuming (I don't agree) the above Story of rejection to be true — can you ask the lady to be stripped naked in public?

You may belive in some crooked justification in Karnas act — the only valid term is — it was an unforgettable unforgiving evil act with absolutely no justification.

I prefer (no supposition) my kid to read every thing related to our glorious culture from the epic to history of great men who shaped us — Chanakya, Vikramaditya, Maharana Pratap and Mahatma Gandhi, Netaji Shubhash Chandra Bose, Bhagat Singh and Chandra Shekhar Azad (I have a rather long and over ambitious list)

Mahabharat! Well our kids are Condemned to live in the world of deceit, where some one may kill you (or get a Quora ban) because they know you are right. They will anyway see Shakuni's everywhere, everyday. They will live Mahabharat.

That said I would value the words of Shiva, Krishna, Parshuram and Indra over Sadguru, especially since all those statements have been backed up by strong evidence.

Ignoring those facts and reference anyone can consider anyone great in their own frame of references.

Did he ever proved himself superior, other than in fables and hypothesis? Then other contemporary warriors like Bhishma and Drona surely disagree with Sadguru.

If Brahmastra was similar to a nuclear weapon then how can two Brahmastra cancel out each other as was generally proposed in Mahabharata?

📅 Aug 19, 2020 10:54 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

First thing first. There is no if. Brahmastra was nothing like nuclear weapon.

Brahmastra was a divine weapon that needed warriors own energy to invoke it unlike modern weapons which anyone could wield. It was available to selected few and not everyone else. Why else warriors couldn't share their knowledge with family and friends?

Moreover these divine weapons not only had their own invocation and execution techniques but also had conscience of their own — so Narayanastra won't destroy those who bow before it and Vaishnavastra doesn't do bidding against Krishna.

To consider them just like nuclear weapon is failing to grasp the significant difference.

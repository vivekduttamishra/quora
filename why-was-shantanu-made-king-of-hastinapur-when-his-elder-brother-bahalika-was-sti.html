Why was Shantanu made king of Hastinapur when his elder brother Bahalika was still alive?

📅 Jun 09, 2020 08:49 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for ask to answer

Shantanu was made the king of Hastinapur bypassing Bahlika is probably the last significant effort made by any successor of the Bharat clan to honor his legacy — prefer capability and merit over the order of the birth. King Pratipa decided to follow merit and Shantanu was crowned.

I have explored this issue in a greater detail in my epic fiction, The Accursed God.

Later Bahlika was made the king of Balkh a small kingdom under influence of Hastinapur.

What was Karna and Draupadi's relationship like?

📅 Mar 18, 2020 08:26 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Draupadi had two emotions for Karna at different phases life and that is based on both references (Vyas Mahabharat Gita Press, KMG translation and Bori) from Mahabharat and rational thinking —

Phase 1 before the great gamble — Apathy

  • Draupadi had no emotion whatsoever. Her Swaymber was designed with Arjun in mind.
  • Swaymber has two story —
    • 1. Popular fiction and KMG — Draupadi didn't allow Karna to participate in Swaymber.
    • 2. Dakshinayat, part of Neelkantha footnote, Bori and Gita press footnote, Mahabharat by Rajgopalachari suggest he failed in competition.
  • Note neither suggest Draupadi had any favourable emotion.

Phase 2 After great gamble — HATE

  • Karna not only abuses Draupadi by word but commands Dussasan to strip her in public (all references except pulp fictions)
  • On several occasion she expressed her trauma, hate and urge for punishment.

Everything else is a far fetched pulp fiction.

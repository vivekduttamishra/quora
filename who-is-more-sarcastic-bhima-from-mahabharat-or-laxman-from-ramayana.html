What is sarcasm?

using remarks that clearly mean the opposite of what you say, in order to hurt someone's feelings or to humorously criticize something

Cambridge Dictionary

I believe neither of them can be considered sarcastic. I don't remember any incident where either spoke without sincerity or Dharma.

I believe in case of Lakshman question talks about Lakshman Parshuram dialogs. But if you read, Lakshman talk straight.

Bhisma too mentions in derogatory term before the start of war, but he stated facts with evidence and was endorsed by likes of Drona.

So I don't see the sarcasm.

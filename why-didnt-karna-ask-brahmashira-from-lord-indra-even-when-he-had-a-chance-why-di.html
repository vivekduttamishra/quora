Why didn't Karna ask Brahmashira from Lord Indra even when he had a chance? Why did he ask weak Vasavi Shakti?

📅 Jan 14, 2022 10:09 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

The exact reason is not given. But that doesn’t matter because the premise of the question is incorrect. Let me start by answering the second part first.

Why did he as weak Vasavi Shakti?

Vasavi Shakti wasn’t a weak weapon. It was strong enough to worry even Shri Krishna. Surya knew it’s power well enough to suggest it to Karna. Karna was happy and content to have the weapon. And most important, that is the only weapon that actually worked for Kauravas. Nothing else ever worked for them.

Why didn't Karna ask Brahmashira from Lord Indra

Here we have to venture into the zone of supposition. However, if you know the epic and understand the nature of divine weapon, the supposition can be well within the range of certainty.

Divine weapons mostly worked under certain conditions —

  • You can’t use it on a weaker opponent — That means it compared the power of both it’s wearer and it’s target.
  • You can’t use it in a hurry without being responsible.

Most important it needed the wearer to be of strong character. It can’t work for wicked person.

Now what option did Karna have? Not being wicked was not an option. And Parshurama never said his Brahmsira will not work for Karna. It was simple Brahmsira would not work for him.

Karna already had that weapon. Only it wasn’t going to work for him.

On the other hand Indra’s weapon had just one condition — you can’t use it till you are left with any other weapon. And Karna hardly had any other weapon of importance. Drona refused him, Parshurama expelled him. And he didn’t have a third guru.

Indra’s weapon was his boon. And it didn’t work on Karna’s own caliber. It suited him perfectly. It was sure to work. And that made it more lethal.

It may not be out of place to remind Karna collected such weapons that doesn’t need his own skill of powers. Another such weapon was Aswhasena, the snake he worshipped for years and finally fired on Arjuna on 17th day.

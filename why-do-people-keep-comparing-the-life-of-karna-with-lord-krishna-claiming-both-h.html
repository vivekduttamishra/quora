Why do people keep comparing the life of Karna with Lord Krishna, claiming both had humble beginnings? Krishna knew his purpose before coming to earth whereas Karna was a human who took decisions based on his experiences and desires in life?

📅 Jan 16, 2021 10:32 PM PST  |  🌐 English

I don’t know who does this stupid comparison. Haven’t seen anyone do this yet. Ved Vyas never did. There is more contrast than similarities between them —

  • Krishna is the root of a tree of righteousness, Karna is the trunk of a tree that is ‘evil Duryodhana’
  • Foster parents raised both Karna and Krishna, but unlike Karna, Krishna was never ashamed of his origin and never blamed his mother for abandoning him without kavach or kundal. He never chose between her two mothers, but accepted them both. Krishna never insulted either of her mothers.
  • Krishna didn’t humilate any women. He accepted thousands of woman who were abandoned by their family because they were captivated by Narkasura. Karna abandons his friend and woman of the family as he flees for his life from Gandharvas.
  • Krishna vows to avenge Draupadi’s humilation. Karna did the humiliation.
  • Krishna was ranchod — He never let his ego come in the way of greater good, Karna refused to see the greater good and rejected Krishna’s peace proposal.
  • Krishna extended the hand to Karna; Karna foresook that hand.
  • Krishna incarnated to uphold Dharma. Karna protected and nurtured Adharma

No. No one compares the two and Ved Vyas never mentioned any comparision.

Who do you like amongst the five Pandavas?

📅 Mar 12, 2022 08:43 PM PST  |  🌐 English

Amongst the five Pandavas, I like best is their brotherhood — they represented the best example of how five finger together becomes a fist.

BROTHERHOOD

Pandavas always remained with philosophy one for all and all for one. Yudhishthira made a mistake in staking them. But they didn’t even blink. That was the brother for each other.

Yudhishthira had the option to ask for the lives of Arjuna or Bhima from Yaksha when four Pandavas died for defying him. But he asks for the life of his half brother. The twins, weren’t going to win the war or make life easier, but for the brothers, it was not profit and loss account that one would make other King out of greed or to appease. He wanted son of his step mother to live.

Arjuna was the greatest and the most powerful warrior of the era. But he still never fought for his own glory. His actions remained dedicated to his family, the society, the humanity. No wonder, even God sought his favor. His victories were offered to all brothers.

The brothers while being with and for each other, also tried to path correct each other, rather than let them perish at their ego and lust. Bhimsena had the courage to call even Yudhishira wrong and challenge him, and at the same time modesty enough accept his younger brother’s sane advise to calm down and value his Dharma over his anger. This would eventually make Yudhishira a better man.

Bhimasena was happy when Kauravas were captured by Gandharvas. He won’t have lifted his finger to protected those heinous creatures. But he did fight Gandharvas, at his brothers command. So did his other brothers.

Do you really want to divide those brothers?

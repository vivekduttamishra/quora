Had Draupadi Really Rejected Karna?

Short answer to this question — NO.

  • Out of 1259 manuscript that BORI consulted 1254 doesn't mention the rejection story. It doesn't exist in —
    • Oldest Nepali manuscript.
    • Entire southern recitation
    • Entire Bengali recitation.
    • This is more than 99.5% that doesn't support rejection theory.
  • It exists in famous Neelkantha edition which is the base of KMG. But Neelkantha also contradicts this by including Karna's failure in Draupadi’s Swayamvara.
  • There is absolutely no cross reference to this story.

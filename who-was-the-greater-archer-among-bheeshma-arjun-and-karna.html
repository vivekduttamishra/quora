Who was the greater Archer among Bheeshma, Arjun and Karna?

📅 Feb 23, 2020 10:39 AM PST  |  🌐 English

If you ask me Karna doesn't stand in the competition. He would be remote 4th or 5th. (in case you forgot Drone and Abhimanyu).

Now coming to Arjun vs Bhisma, there is ample evidence in favour of the grand Bhisma. His 10 days battle was a nightmere for Pandavas who had lost all hopes for victory and finally Arjun used Shikandi as a shield to disarm him.

So Bhisma was a greater archer and a greater warrior who had defeated even bhagwan Parshuram.

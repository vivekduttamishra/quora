Lord Krishna stopped time to explain all 700 slokas of Bhagavad Gita. Did he really stop time? If yes, how? What is the real meaning of that?

📅 Mar 28, 2020 01:50 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Sometime we put too much importance to literal words such as stopping time, and in essence we defocus the main section (in this case the real teachings of Gita). Such questions create unnecessary doubts. And these doubts let us doubt other aspects of the teaching also.

While with Lord Krishna around, it is not too much of a stretch to imagine that he could have stopped time, it may very well be a metaphor. Arjuna benefited from Gita teachings others ignored and remained in their time doing whatever they were doing. During Gita Arjuna was totally absorved in discussion as if what is happening around didn’t matter.

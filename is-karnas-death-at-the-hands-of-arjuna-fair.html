Is Karna's death at the hands of Arjuna fair?

📅 Mar 21, 2020 10:55 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Well fairness is a vague term. It was a justice delivered.

  1. Was it fair for Karna abuse Draupadi and command stripping in public? → This reason alone suffices for killing Karna.
  2. Shouldn’t someone who conspires burning an entire family alive with a hapless lady?
  3. Was it fair for Karna to mock Pandavas in exile?
  4. Was it fair for Karna to kill Gahtotkatch or Abhimanyu?
  5. Was it fair for Pandavas to suffer all their lives?

Is it fair to expect a fair death for someone who never himself was fair?

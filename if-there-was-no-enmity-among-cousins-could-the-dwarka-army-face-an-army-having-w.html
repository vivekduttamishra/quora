If there was no enmity among cousins, Could the Dwarka army face an army having warriors like Bhishma, Drona, Karna, Ashwathama, 5 Pandavas, 100 Kauravas and their allies like Bhagadatta, the Bhurishravas, Drupada, Jarasandha and Eklavya?

Short answer: That was NEVER going to happen.

Forget the fact, that Krishna was invincible against everyone, there would have been no situation where Pandavas had fought against Krishna and specially siding with evils like Jarasandha. It was equally unlikely that Drona and Drupada fighting under the same banner.

The relation between Krishna and Pandavas wasn’t a relationship of convenience or because of the rivalry between Kaurvas and Pandavas. They were together because they knew what is Dharma.

Now let us tackle the first part of the question — What would have been the condition in which the enmity between cousins would not be existed? I see only one — Kauravas would have accepted what they already knew, the rights of Pandavas over the throne. But that would have made Kauravas righteous people. And had they been righteous it was unlikely that they would have sided with the likes of Jarasandha.

So as I see if there were no enmity between the cousins it may be battle with one side having Jarasandha, Shishupala, Bhagdatta, Rukmi, Eklavya and Karna fighting against Hastinapur, Dwarka, with Panchala.

Now you see in this alternate universe, sidelined by Drona and with no support from Duryodhana (as there is no enmity between cousins) Karna would have found not much prospect in Hastinapur because of his open rivalry against Arjuna and in all probability he would have sided with Jarasandha, where he had a chance to fight against Arjuna.

Drona may have abstained as he would not fight under same banner as Drupada.

But I guess we all know this is one big supposition that wasn’t going to happen.

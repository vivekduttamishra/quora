What if Karna defeated Arjuna?

📅 Feb 22, 2020 08:48 PM PST  |  🌐 English

This is one of those wishful thinking that starts with what if…but it was not to be —

Karna never defeated Arjuna — not once!

Arjuna bested him — again and again — in Virat, in Kurukshetra. Arjuna won Gandharvas and freed Duryodhan — Karna? He fled.

How could have he won Arjun with Krishna as his guard, when he can't win him alone?

How could Karna have won him who was blessed by Shiva?

But yes if he could have won several outcomes were possible

  1. Bhimsena would have killed him who had bested him several times in Kurukshetra war and spared him for Arjuna to ulfil his oath.
  2. Krishna would have slain him. Remember he spared Bhisma only on request from Arjuna. With Arjuna gone…

But as I said, it was not to be…

Had there been shakuni in place of duryodhan and he had the chance to choose between Shri Krishna and narayani sena, whom did he choose as per your understanding & why do you think so?

📅 Oct 28, 2020 10:37 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Hi, thanks for the A2A.

This question assumes Shakuni to be as intelligent and as cunning as shown in the TV shows. But no such reference is available in Vyas Mahabharata.

As per Vyasa Bharata, while Shakuni was wicked and a partner in crime in all Duryodhana's conspiracy, he mostly had the role of a minion and not the brain which was mostly Duryodhana or Karna.

The only occasion Shakuni becomes prominent was the devising of the great gamble.

So I don't see Shakuni making any move more brilliant than Duryodhana.

Honestly Duryodhana's choice was logical — an army against a single person and no one could have been wiser to chose Krishna, unless he wanted them to.

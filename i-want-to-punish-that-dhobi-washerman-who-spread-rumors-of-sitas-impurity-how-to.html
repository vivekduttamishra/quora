I want to punish that dhobi (washerman) who spread rumors of Sita's impurity. How to punish him?

📅 Feb 14, 2021 11:42 PM PST  |  🌐 English

That is a marvellous idea. We all must, the way Sri Rama did.

But first, we must understand that, only that dhobi didn’t deserve the punishment. He just represented a mindset. He represented the rotten mindset of a section of the society.

That washerman is dead, Rama and Sita have returned to their abode. But you don’t need to time travel to punish the culprit, because the culprit lives on even today — the same rotten mindset.

And a mindset that outlived the era by several thousands of years is not that easy to destroy. It sits in so many minds. First, we must attack such mindset germinating within us. We can pledge, we won't we like that Dhobi ever. We won’t ask any Sita to be banished on baseless acquisition. We would protect her, and give her a society that Sita deserved. We can pledge Sita won’t be buried either in ground or shame ever again!

Next stage is to attack him and purge him from the minds of our family and friends. Keep widening the circle of influence and maybe one day that washerman would be banished from our society.

HOW WOULD BE KNOW THAT FISHERMAN IS PUNISHED?

When the fisherman would be banished, we would truly realize that Rama had actually punished that mindset years ago by his suffering and that of Sita. Once we succeed in eradicating that mindset, society would truly understand Rama and Sita and would never blame Rama for banishing Sita.

We truly need to destroy and banish that mindset, that fisherman represented.

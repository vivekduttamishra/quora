Did Draupadi really reject Karna? What is some proof?

📅 Jan 24, 2021 10:54 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Short Answer: NO.

Detailed Answer with all proofs: https://www.quora.com/q/dqqdyatpbbobowgd/Had-Draupadi-really-stopped-Karna-from-Participating-in-her-Swyamber-Source-Reference-The-Mahabharata-Project-Main-Ar?ch=10&share=66bc3219

Summary

  • Only 5 out of 1259 ancient script collected by BORI CE includes this story. This story is absent from oldest Nepali script, entire Bengali edition, Sharda edition and most prominent and complete southern editions.
  • Neelkantha edition (foundation for KMG) is one of the most prominent edition to have included this story. However, it explicitly includes Karna’s failure to string the bow, thus contradicting the story.
  • There is absolutely no cross-reference of Draupadi rejecting Karna anywhere in any epic. Noone ever condemned Draupadi for rejecting Karna as it didn’t happen.
  • It doesn’t match with the narratives and character. Draupadi was a docile character. She didn’t object Arjuna from participating who was an ordinary Brahmin. She goes with her quitely. She doesn’t object polyandry.
  • Prof. Vishnu Suthankar, the chief editor of BORI, based on his extensive research, in his editorial preface categorically mentioned that this story is later add-on and not the part of the authentic text.

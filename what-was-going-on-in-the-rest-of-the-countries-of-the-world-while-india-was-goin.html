📅 May 15, 2020 09:33 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

These questions are almost theme questions — same question reborn with slight different wordings. I have answered it a few times such as — Vivek Dutta Mishra (विवेक दत्त मिश्रा)'s answer to When the Mahabharata and Ramayana were taking place in India, what was happening in the rest of the world? Why did the Gods mentioned in the Mahabharata and Ramayana confine their actions to India's boundaries? Are they just made-up stories?

This question, however, is more polite and apparently born out of curiosity. So let me add a few more points —

  2. Where civilization existed, they didn't follow the superior technique of Shruti and smriti, to preserve those knowledge.   4. We in India, too, suffered such attack and they did prove almost grave — they tried to cast aside our epics as mythology and worst, many actually started believing them and questioning the relevance of our own culture and heritage. But because of our strong faith we thrawt the attack.

Today Indian sanatan culture, epic and traditions are only ancient culture surviving. So how would you find what was happening to that other part of the world which had either not entered into civilization or is now wiped out from existence to tell its tale.

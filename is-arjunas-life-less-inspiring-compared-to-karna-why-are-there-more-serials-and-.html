Is Arjuna's life less inspiring compared to Karna? Why are there more serials and movies about Karna but none about Arjuna?

📅 May 26, 2020 12:57 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Wow! What an excellent deduction. Certainly Arjuna is less inspiring for writers than Arjuna — they don't have much scope for their creativity or fables. Much has been said about in passed 5000 years.

And yes Arjuna's life isn't inspiring —

  • He was so predictable — won every single battle of his life. So boring!
  • As a student, he was hard working, disciplined, focused and dutiful — so untrending! Today we need someone to break the lock of deans office to steal exam papers. How would Arjun be a role model? Yes, Karna is really inspiring.
  • Arjuna suffered years in exile, worked hard to please Shiva. Do we need an inspiration from such a fool when we can be inspired by someone who became a king just by a good marketing and glimics and continue to rule all his life.
  • Krishna endorsed him, Shiva and Hanuman blessed him. Bhisma, Drona everyone liked him. He doesn't need a creative writer to improve his personality and their condemnation won't stand. Best stay clear.
  • There are few characters who are in dire need for a good P. R. to hide their failures and justify their actions. So writers put their effort polishing them with their baseless narratives, mostly borrowed from similar baseless narratives.

Oh yes, Arjuna as a character may not interest those story tellers who are terrified of the truth. It's not Arjuna's life that is less inspiring, it's their reluctance to go against current trends.

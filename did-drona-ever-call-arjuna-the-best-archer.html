Did Drona ever call Arjuna the best archer?

📅 Aug 09, 2022 10:27 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Drona called Arjuna as the best archer several times, both directly and indirectly. However, he didn’t use this term during the passing out exhibition, where he called Arjuna as the younger brother of Indra and dearer than his own son. Many use this words as a pretext to say, Karna entered the ceremony because Drona proclaimed Arjuna as the best. NOT TRUE.

Apart from Drona, Bhishma, Krishna, Ashwatthama, Karna himself and above all Mahadeva Shiva called Arjuna as the best.

Which were the top 10 deadliest weapons used in Mahabharata and Ramayana?

📅 Mar 10, 2020 02:47 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Rajkumar Singh Thanks for inviting me to answer this question. I have answered a very similar question recently here: Vivek Dutta Mishra (विवेक दत्त मिश्रा)'s answer to What are the top 10 most powerful weapons in Mahabharata?

As such answer will be basically similar — I wont actually grade the weapons of Ramayana or Mahabharata. I have actually a question which I feel is more relevant —

  1. Why were they called Divyastras?
  2. Who could wield them?
  3. What form of energies they used?
  4. Was it really possible for all and sundry to wield them.

Let me remind you once more that Mahabharat expanded from Jaya (8000 slokas) to Mahabharat (90000 slokas). The core context didn’t have such a detailed description of each celestial weapons as they have expanded. What is more valuable in my understanding is understanding the divine nature of those weapons for which we get a glance of Arjuna’s grand effort in archiving Pashupatastra from Lord Shiva!

Now I have my perception of Divyastras which are not inline with expanded Mahabharat’s interpretation, so it is not fair that I should add my interpretation to a question which is seeking an answer from the epic itself.

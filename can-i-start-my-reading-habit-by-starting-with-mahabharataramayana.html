Can I start my reading habit by starting with Mahabharata/Ramayana?

📅 Sep 20, 2020 12:05 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

You surely can start your reading habit by reading either of these two great epics. But I would recommend start by reading their abridged and non-fiction version written by unbiased scholars like Shri Rajagopalachari.

The unabridged versions like BORI of KMG may be overwhelming. They are two big and may dampen your zeal of developing a reading habit. Moreover, the narration of KMG is not contemporary and may not help you develop the reading habit.

The fictionalized version, while may prove good for reading, may take you farther away from the facts as they are often character oriented rather than epic oriented. That said, I would like to take this opportunity to introduce my epic fiction The Accursed God which is my take on this great epic. While it is an undeniable fiction, I have tried to do the justice to epic rather than to individual characters. This is my attempt to showcase the great Indian epic and great ancient Indian culture. You may like it.

Moving away from Ramayana and Mahabharata, you may also try out some great short stories by authors like Munshi Premchand in hindi and Jeffrey Archer in english. They may help you develop your reading habit.

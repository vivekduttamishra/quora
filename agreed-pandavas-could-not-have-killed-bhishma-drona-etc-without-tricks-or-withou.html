The question starts with a WRONG premise where answering it would mean agreeing to the first part, which is an incorrect supposition and goes to an impossible corner, which never was even considered by any Kaurava.

First, was it impossible to kill Bhishma and Drona without any trick?

Go to Matasya battle and you have your answer. Every Kuru warrior was there and at one point no one was standing. They all would have been killed if not for the compassion of Arjuna.

And that same compassion prevailed even in Kurukshetra as the battle prolonged, each day Kauravas losing the day, Drona and Bhishma standing and yet Arjuna won't kill his grandfather and Guru. Why else did Krishha rushed to kill Bhishma?

Why couldn't he protect Jaydradh from Arjuna? And why did Arujna rushed to save him from dhristiduymna? Because…

Could Kauravas have killed Arjuna?

Well forget what Krishna said, or Parshurama said, or Shiva said or what actually happened, again and again, everytime, the simple answer is No Kauravas even dared to dream. Not only they didn't dare to dream, they didn't dare even to try form a vyuh to that. He was the only one for whom a dedicated task force of 14000 warriors was formed. And… they all died. And it wasn't without deceit.

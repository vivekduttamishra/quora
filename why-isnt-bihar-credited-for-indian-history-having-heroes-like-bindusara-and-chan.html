Why isn’t Bihar credited for Indian history, having heroes like Bindusara and Chandragupta 2?

📅 Dec 12, 2020 04:22 AM PST  |  🌐 English

Bihar has enjoyed a glorified position from pre-historic place. Look forward to it on India’s national flag and coins and look for it on the uniforms of the defender of the nation. It is the birthplace for civilization and empires. It is the origin of Buddhas enlightenment; it is the land where Alexander couldn’t dare to enter with his armies. It is the land of Chanakya and first historic empire. It is the land of Ashoka. And it is the land of Guru Gobind Singh. It is the land where Mahatma Gandhi started his first movement, and it is the land who son presided over that constituent assembly that gave Independent India its constitution.

And one need to be blind, not credit Bihar for its contribution.

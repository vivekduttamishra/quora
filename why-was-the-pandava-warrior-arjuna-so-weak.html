Why was the Pandava warrior Arjuna so weak?

📅 May 19, 2020 12:03 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Oh! Yes Pandava's side “Arjuna” was not just weak and but even a bigger fool why else —

  • They followed Dharma despite Arjuna getting Pashupatastra from great Shiva himself? And who but a pathetic weak hope to win blessings of Shiva?
  • He won the war without ever using the most potent of all weapons — Pashupatastra?
  • They defeated Gandharvas to rescue that Duryodhana who always tormented them? Surely they must be afraid. Right?
  • He was Drona's most favorite? Surely Guru always favors the dumbest and weakest. Right?
  • Drona waited three days to form Chakravyuh? Naturally he was worried the weak Arjuna will fail same way as he failed Gurudakshina war all those years ago.
  • He alone cast aside his bow even before the start of war and got blessed with Gita?

So who were the braves?

  • Those who lost every single battle against him — Gurudakshina war, Swaymber battle, Gandharvas battle, Virat and finally Kurukshetra?
  • Those who cheated their own family, friends and Gurus?
  • Those who used deceit to poisen, burn, enslave and mock others?
  • Those who were vanquished along with their numerically superior army?

Yes Arjuna was so weak that we installed Arjuna award to celebrate his weakness. May we all be as weak as him.

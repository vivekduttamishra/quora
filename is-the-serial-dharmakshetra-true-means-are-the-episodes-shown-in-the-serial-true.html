Is the serial Dharmakshetra True means are the episodes shown in the serial True related to Epic Mahabharata?

📅 May 26, 2021 02:29 AM PDT  |  🌐 English

Dharmakshetra has an unique presentation and I must applaud for the presentation. That said, not only the context is imaginary, but a lot of incidents mentioned there is work of fictions — for example the five arrow story that Bhishma gave to Duryodhana and Duryodhana gave to Arjuna. The story has no base. There are many other such stories.

My Recommendation — look for the messages that you may find useful. Don’t take them as authentic.

Personally I liked a few parts of that series.

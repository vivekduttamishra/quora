Was Lord Rama really the greatest warrior ever born? Who is the warrior who can defeat him?

📅 Jun 19, 2020 12:16 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks @Sujal Singh for ask to answer. Ram was indeed one of the greatest warrior ever born and Krishna confirms it when he says — Among weilders of weapons, I am Ram.

But a great warrior is not someone who never looses a battle, he is someone who never looses himself in the face of adversity. Take the example of Maharana Pratap — did he succeed in freeing Chittoor? Did he defeat Akbar conclusively? No. Was he a great warrior? Absolutely — one of the greatest warrior in history.

Ravana abducted Sita under his guard. Wasn't that a defeat for Ram? What makes him great is he won his personal turmoil and prioritized Jatayu and Sugriv's pain.

He crossed over to Lanka and yet offered Ravana and Lanka, a last chance for peace. That's what makes him a great warrior.

He wins Lanka, but does not gloat over his victory, rather passes the baton to Vibhishan. How many warriors we know who are so polite in victory?

Who could win Ram?

Well you can win against him. Anyone with true devotion can make him surrender. Bharat certainly did. Hanuman did.

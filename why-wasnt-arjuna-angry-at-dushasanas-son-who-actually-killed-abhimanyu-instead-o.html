Why wasn't Arjuna angry at Dushasana's son who actually killed Abhimanyu instead of Jaydrath?

📅 Apr 28, 2022 11:39 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

First things first. It would be wrong to say Duhshasan’s son killed Abhimanyu. No one other the most recent social media propaganda says so in an attempt to exonerate one heinous villain of that time, which one would be clear if you read other answers of the proponent of this absurd theory. The epic itself doesn’t say so. It clearly mentions seven Maharathi’s were involved in this gruesome act.

Duhshashana’s son indeed had landed the last blow, last among the thousands of blows that had landed on the brave Abhimanyu. And If it was really Duhshashana’s son who killed Abhimanyu, he must have been the bravest of the Kuru warriors because others cowards kept running from Abhimanyu. Again and Again. They ran seeing their brother killed, and son killed. They were cowards, no doubt, but that doesn’t make Duhshashana’s son brave or of any significance. Do you know why? Answer lies in the question. Read the question again. Arjuna doesn’t direct his anger against Duhshashana’s son who was too insignificant to be even named. But then, you see, Arjuna didn’t follow social media. He understood who were real guilty.

But then, the question may arise, why did Arjuna redirect his anger only on Jaidradh? First because Jaidradh was one person who had robbed Abhimanyu of his chance to have a fair battle. He prevented armed help from reaching to Abhimanyu. Had Abhimanyu got a fair chance…

But it would be a wrong conclusion, based only on words, to assume Jaidradh was the only one who got the burnt of Pandava’s anger. Interestingly Jaidradh had prevent armed help from reaching Abhimanyu and next day the entire army couldn’t stop Arjuna from reaching Jaidradh. It wasn’t just a punishment to Jaidradh alone, but a reminder that non one is beyond the reach of Arjuna’s arrow. Same day as Jaidradh died, Arjuna announced that he would kill Karna’s son. In front of him. No deceit. No need to engage Karna elsewhere. No need of any Jaidradh to prevent help reaching his son. And he killed Karna’s son in front of him on day 17. Karna could do nothing. Did Karuavas even dare to kill Abhimanyu in front of Arjuna? Drona mercilessly killed the son of his so called favorite pupil and tried to prevent Arjuna from reaching Jaidradh knowing well had he succeeded Arjuna would have died. Guess what, that merciless guru died delusional lamenting the death of his son, who wasn’t dead. And Arjuna indeed tried to protect him.

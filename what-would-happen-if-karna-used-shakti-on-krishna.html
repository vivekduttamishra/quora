What would happen if Karna used Shakti on Krishna?

📅 Aug 15, 2020 01:17 PM PDT  |  🌐 English

Thanks for the A2A.

If Karna had used Indra's weapon on Krishna it would have met the same fate as Bhagdatta's Viashnavastra or Ashwattgama's Narayanastra or Brahmastra he hurled at unborn child of Uttara — Fissss.

For no weapon is greater than mighty God incarnate on his mission to establish Dharma. Indra and Krishna had two face offs earlier, none in favour of Indra — Govardhan and Khandava incident.

What do you think, would a weapon without its true Master would have done? Probably become another garland in Krishna's shoulder.

At the end of the day it's not what weapons could do, it's what Krishna wishes. And when he was ready, no divine weapon was needed — an ordinary bow from an ordinary hunter.
